# Bhavas (Houses)

The birth chart is divided into twelve houses (Bhavas), each representing different areas of life.

## The Twelve Houses

### 1st House - [<span className="hindi">लग्न</span> (Lagna) / Ascendant](/bhavas/first)
- **Significator**: <span className="hindi">सूर्य</span> (Sun)
- **Represents**: Self, personality, physical body, overall life path
- **Nature**: <span className="hindi">तनु भाव</span> (Tanu Bhava - Body House)
- **Classification**: <span className="hindi">केंद्र</span> (Kendra/Angular), <span className="hindi">त्रिकोण</span> (Trikona/Trinal)

### 2nd House - [<span className="hindi">धन भाव</span> (Dhana Bhava)](/bhavas/second)
- **Significator**: <span className="hindi">गुरु</span> (Jupiter)
- **Represents**: Wealth, family, speech, food, early education
- **Nature**: <span className="hindi">धन भाव</span> (Dhana Bhava - Wealth House)
- **Classification**: <span className="hindi">मारक</span> (Marak - Death-inflicting)

### 3rd House - [<span className="hindi">सहज भाव</span> (Sahaj Bhava)](/bhavas/third)
- **Significator**: <span className="hindi">मंगल</span> (Mars)
- **Represents**: Siblings, courage, communication, short travels, skills
- **Nature**: <span className="hindi">सहज भाव</span> (Sahaj Bhava - Siblings House)
- **Classification**: <span className="hindi">उपचय</span> (Upachaya - Growth House)

### 4th House - [<span className="hindi">सुख भाव</span> (Sukha Bhava)](/bhavas/fourth)
- **Significator**: <span className="hindi">चंद्र</span> (Moon)
- **Represents**: Mother, home, property, vehicles, emotions, education
- **Nature**: <span className="hindi">सुख भाव</span> (Sukha Bhava - Happiness House)
- **Classification**: <span className="hindi">केंद्र</span> (Kendra/Angular)

### 5th House - [<span className="hindi">पुत्र भाव</span> (Putra Bhava)](/bhavas/fifth)
- **Significator**: <span className="hindi">गुरु</span> (Jupiter)
- **Represents**: Children, creativity, intelligence, romance, past life karma
- **Nature**: <span className="hindi">पुत्र भाव</span> (Putra Bhava - Children House)
- **Classification**: <span className="hindi">त्रिकोण</span> (Trikona/Trinal)

### 6th House - [<span className="hindi">रिपु भाव</span> (Ripu Bhava)](/bhavas/sixth)
- **Significator**: <span className="hindi">मंगल</span> (Mars), <span className="hindi">शनि</span> (Saturn)
- **Represents**: Enemies, diseases, debts, service, daily work
- **Nature**: <span className="hindi">रिपु भाव</span> (Ripu Bhava - Enemy House)
- **Classification**: <span className="hindi">दुष्टान</span> (Dusthana - Difficult House), <span className="hindi">उपचय</span> (Upachaya)

### 7th House - [<span className="hindi">कलत्र भाव</span> (Kalatra Bhava)](/bhavas/seventh)
- **Significator**: <span className="hindi">शुक्र</span> (Venus)
- **Represents**: Spouse, marriage, partnerships, business
- **Nature**: <span className="hindi">कलत्र भाव</span> (Kalatra Bhava - Spouse House)
- **Classification**: <span className="hindi">केंद्र</span> (Kendra/Angular), <span className="hindi">मारक</span> (Marak)

### 8th House - [<span className="hindi">आयु भाव</span> (Ayur Bhava)](/bhavas/eighth)
- **Significator**: <span className="hindi">शनि</span> (Saturn)
- **Represents**: Longevity, transformation, occult, inheritance, sudden events
- **Nature**: <span className="hindi">आयु भाव</span> (Ayur Bhava - Longevity House)
- **Classification**: <span className="hindi">दुष्टान</span> (Dusthana - Difficult House)

### 9th House - [<span className="hindi">भाग्य भाव</span> (Bhagya Bhava)](/bhavas/ninth)
- **Significator**: <span className="hindi">गुरु</span> (Jupiter), <span className="hindi">सूर्य</span> (Sun)
- **Represents**: Father, guru, fortune, dharma, long travels, higher education
- **Nature**: <span className="hindi">भाग्य भाव</span> (Bhagya Bhava - Fortune House)
- **Classification**: <span className="hindi">त्रिकोण</span> (Trikona/Trinal)

### 10th House - [<span className="hindi">कर्म भाव</span> (Karma Bhava)](/bhavas/tenth)
- **Significator**: <span className="hindi">सूर्य</span> (Sun), <span className="hindi">बुध</span> (Mercury), <span className="hindi">गुरु</span> (Jupiter), <span className="hindi">शनि</span> (Saturn)
- **Represents**: Career, status, reputation, karma
- **Nature**: <span className="hindi">कर्म भाव</span> (Karma Bhava - Career House)
- **Classification**: <span className="hindi">केंद्र</span> (Kendra/Angular)

### 11th House - [<span className="hindi">लाभ भाव</span> (Labha Bhava)](/bhavas/eleventh)
- **Significator**: <span className="hindi">गुरु</span> (Jupiter)
- **Represents**: Gains, income, friends, aspirations, elder siblings
- **Nature**: <span className="hindi">लाभ भाव</span> (Labha Bhava - Gains House)
- **Classification**: <span className="hindi">उपचय</span> (Upachaya - Growth House)

### 12th House - [<span className="hindi">व्यय भाव</span> (Vyaya Bhava)](/bhavas/twelfth)
- **Significator**: <span className="hindi">शनि</span> (Saturn)
- **Represents**: Losses, expenses, foreign lands, spirituality, moksha, isolation
- **Nature**: <span className="hindi">व्यय भाव</span> (Vyaya Bhava - Loss House)
- **Classification**: <span className="hindi">दुष्टान</span> (Dusthana - Difficult House)

## Classification of Houses

### <span className="hindi">केंद्र स्थान</span> (Kendra Sthan) - Angular Houses

**Houses: 1, 4, 7, 10**

- <span className="hindi">विष्णु स्थान</span> (Vishnu Sthans) - Important bhavas
- Most powerful houses
- Provide strength and stability
- **1st House (Lagna)**: Is both <span className="hindi">केंद्र</span> (Kendra) and <span className="hindi">त्रिकोण</span> (Trikona)

### <span className="hindi">त्रिकोण स्थान</span> (Trikona Sthana) - Trinal Houses

**Houses: 1, 5, 9**

- <span className="hindi">लक्ष्मी स्थान</span> (Laxmi Sthan) - Auspicious houses
- <span className="hindi">शुभ स्थान</span> (Shubh Sthans) - They are auspicious houses
- Auspicious houses representing dharma, fortune, and self
- **Purification Power**: The lords of 1, 5, 9 can purify any planet they associate with, even if that lord is the lord of 6, 8, or 12
- **1, 5, 10**: Are houses of <span className="hindi">कृपा</span> of <span className="hindi">राजा</span> (Kripa of Raja) - Grace of the King

### <span className="hindi">दुष्टान</span> (Dusthana) - Difficult Houses

**Houses: 6, 8, 12**

- Also known as <span className="hindi">त्रिषडाय</span> (Trisadaya) - Malefic Houses
- Challenging areas that bring growth through difficulty
- These houses have negative energy
- They can pollute other planets that come in contact with them
- Planets placed here or their lords can bring challenges and obstacles

### <span className="hindi">उपचय</span> (Upachaya) - Growth Houses

**Houses: 3, 6, 10, 11**

- Improve with time
- Malefics do well here

### <span className="hindi">मारक</span> (Maraka) - Death-inflicting Houses

**Houses: 2, 7**

- Can cause health issues during dashas
