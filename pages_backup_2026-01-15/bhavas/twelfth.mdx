# 12th Bhava - <span className="hindi">व्यय भाव</span> (Vyaya Bhava)

## Basic Information

- **Sanskrit Name**: <span className="hindi">व्यय भाव</span> (Vyaya Bhava - House of Losses)
- **Significator**: <span className="hindi">गुरु</span> (Jupiter), <span className="hindi">शनि</span> (Saturn), <span className="hindi">केतु</span> (Ketu)
- **Classification**: <span className="hindi">त्रिषडाय</span> (Trisadaya/Malefic), <span className="hindi">दुष्टान</span> (Dusthana - Difficult House)
- **Essence**: "Exit Gate" of the material world
- **Planet**: Ruled by **<span className="hindi">गुरु</span> (Jupiter)** (natural chart – Pisces)
