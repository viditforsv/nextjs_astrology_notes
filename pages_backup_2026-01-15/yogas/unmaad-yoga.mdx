# <span className="hindi">उन्माद योग</span> (Unmaad Yoga)

## Overview

<span className="hindi">बुध</span> (Mercury) + <span className="hindi">राहु</span> (Rahu) combination gives obsession. It is called <span className="hindi">उन्माद योग</span> (Unmaad Yoga).

## Components

- **<span className="hindi">बुध</span> (Mercury)**: Intelligence
- **<span className="hindi">राहु</span> (Rahu)**: Obsession
