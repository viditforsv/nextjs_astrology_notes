## Jupiter in 2nd House

- **House meaning**: Family, values, speech, initial sanskara.
- **If benefic & unpolluted**:
  - From a cultured, wise family (not necessarily wealthy).
  - Speaks only when needed; talks about knowledge. 

- **If afflicted**:
  - Gives unsolicited advice (“gyan baantna”), excessive counseling habit.

- **Taste & Preferences**:
  - Fond of sweets, juices, fruits.

- **Wealth**:
  - Earns through lawful, knowledge-based work (law, teaching, judging, consultancy). #profession 
  - Steady, gradual growth in wealth — once given, Jupiter does not take back.

- **Marriage impact**:
  - Not considered good for marriage — sits 8th from 7th house (Staan Hani Jiwa principle), can harm marriage, esp. in Gemini Lagna.
	  - in Mithun lagna
		- Jupiter is 7th lord and 12th lord
		- Jupiter in 2nd house is 8th from 7th that too uccha

- **Aspects from 2nd House**:

  - 6th: Overcomes enemies/accusations.
  - 8th: Reduces suffering, gives spiritual tendencies, desire for astrology/meditation.
  - 10th: Career grows gradually; earns through voice/advisory work.

- **Affliction effects**:
	- Fatty liver possibility (due to 6th house aspect).
	  - Loss of family harmony, money loss.

- **Unique family role**: Resolves disputes, even if youngest — source of solutions.
