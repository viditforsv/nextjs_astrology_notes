## Characteristics

Bharani represents the power to take away and transform. It's associated with birth, death, and rebirth - the complete cycle of existence.

### Positive Traits
- Strong and determined
- Creative and artistic
- Loyal and devoted
- Natural leadership abilities
- Talented in arts and music
- Fearless and courageous
- Deep understanding of life and death

### Negative Traits
- Can be extreme in behavior
- Possessive and jealous
- May struggle with anger
- Tendency towards indulgence
- Can be manipulative
- May face moral dilemmas

## Ruling Deity: Yama

Yama is the god of death and dharma (righteousness). He represents:
- Justice and fairness
- Discipline and restraint
- The cycle of life and death
- Moral judgment
