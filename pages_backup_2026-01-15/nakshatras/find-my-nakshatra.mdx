## Understanding Navtara Chakra

### The 9 Navtara Chakras

1. **Janma** (Very Good) - Birth/Origin
   - Good time to start any important work
   - Represents new beginnings and favorable conditions

2. **Sampat** - Wealth/Consistency
   - Good time to start something related to Money
   - Represents material gains and stability

3. **Vipat** - Danger ⚠️
   - No travel. No Risk
   - Period of potential obstacles and challenges

4. **Kshema** - Well Being
   - Good Time To Start Health Related activities
   - Represents protection and welfare

5. **Pratyaari** - Obstacle ⚠️
   - Avoid anything New thing. Avoid Taking Risks
   - Period of potential opposition and difficulties

6. **Sadhana** - Achievement
   - Good Time To Start Meditation, Sadhana, Spiritual
   - Represents spiritual practice and accomplishment

7. **Naidhana** - Death ⛔
   - Don't start any new work. Don't Take Risk
   - Most inauspicious period, requires extreme caution

8. **Mitra** - Friend
   - Good To start new thing.
   - Represents friendly and supportive conditions

9. **Param Mitra** (Very Good) - Very Good Friend
   - Good To start new thing.
   - Most favorable period for new beginnings
