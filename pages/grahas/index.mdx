# Planets in Vedic Astrology (Navagrahas)

In Vedic Astrology, nine planets (Navagrahas) are considered, including two shadow planets (Rahu and Ketu).

## Classification

### Natural Benefics
- Jupiter (greatest benefic)
- Venus
- Mercury (when well-placed)
- Waxing Moon

### Natural Malefics
- Saturn (greatest malefic)
- Mars
- Rahu
- Ketu
- Sun
- Waning Moon

### Temporal Benefics/Malefics
The nature of planets also changes based on house rulership for each ascendant.

## Important Planetary States

- **Exaltation (Uchcha)**: Planet at maximum strength
- **Moolatrikona**: Own sign with special dignity
- **Own Sign (Swarashi)**: Comfortable placement
- **Friend's Sign**: Positive placement
- **Enemy's Sign**: Challenging placement
- **Debilitation (Neecha)**: Planet at minimum strength
