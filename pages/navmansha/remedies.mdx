# Navmansha Remedies

## General Principle

**Paap graha ke upaay dasha se pehle karne hai taaki dasha main losses na ho.**

Remedies should be performed **before** the dasha of malefic planets to prevent losses during the dasha period.

**Remedy karni chaiye D9 H1/H5/H9 ki.**

---

## Remedies for Specific Houses

### D9 1st House (Lagna)

If malefic planets are in D9 Lagna, remedies are needed to balance the negative effects.

**Note:** D9H1 pe paap graha hai to iska matlaab aap aana nahi chahte the, aate time mata ko kastha hua hoga.

---

### D9 5th House

Malefics in D9 5th house require remedies to ease internal struggles and subconscious difficulties.

---

### D9 9th House

Malefics in D9 9th house require specific remedies because luck is outside your control.

**Important:** Unlike the 5th house (where meditation works), malefics here *require* specific remedies.

**The "99" Rule:** A malefic planet here acts like the snake at 99 in "Snakes and Ladders." It hits when you are close to success.

#### Planet-Specific Remedies for D9 9th House

- **<span className="hindi">शनि</span> (Saturn) in 9th:** Worship Goddess Devi (Sri Suktam)
- **<span className="hindi">राहु</span> (Rahu) in 9th:** Worship Lord Vishnu
- **<span className="hindi">मंगल</span> (Mars) in 9th:** Worship Rudra
- **<span className="hindi">सूर्य</span> (Sun) in 9th:** It is safe (Karaka of the 9th house)
- **Exalted Malefics:** They act like Vishnu/Protectors (Good result)

---

### D9 10th House

Malefics in D9 10th house indicate past karma related to money. They are here to *take* money (past debts). Voluntary donation is essential.

**The Rule:** "He who gives will also take." If you don't donate, the planet will take the money forcefully.

---

## Timing for Remedies

- **<span className="hindi">राहु</span> (Rahu) Remedies:** Perform during <span className="hindi">राहु</span> (Rahu) kaal

---

## Important Notes

1. **Voluntary Donation:** If you don't donate voluntarily, the malefic planet will take the money forcefully through losses/medical bills.

2. **Exception:** If the malefic is in its **Own Sign (Swaraashi)** or **Exalted**, it will *not* take the money away (e.g., <span className="hindi">शनि</span> (Saturn) in <span className="hindi">कुंभ</span> (Kumbha) in 10th).

3. **D9H5/H9:** Paap graha dokha dete hai. Jhoote guru se bachna chaiye (Malefics deceive. Avoid fake gurus).

4. **Patient Remedies:** Patient ko touch kara ke koi bhi daan kar sakta hai (can perform any donation by touching the patient).
