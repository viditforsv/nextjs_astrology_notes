## Mythology

### Birth & Background
- Son of Rishi Angiras (one of Brahma's 8 sons)
- Gained spiritual knowledge from his father
- Performed severe penance
- Became Guru of the Devas (Gods)

### Two Cosmic Groups
- **Sur (Devas)**: Brihaspati is their Guru
- **Asur (Demons)**: Shukracharya is their Guru

### Key Mythological Story
- Shukracharya went to perform penance to Lord Shiva
- Brihaspati took disguise of Shukracharya to enter Asuras' camp
- A female named Jayanthi married "Shukracharya" (actually Brihaspati in disguise) for 10 years with condition of seclusion
- During 10 years, Brihaspati trained Asuras, confusing their loyalty
- Shukracharya cursed the Asuras out of rage upon return
- Brihaspati revealed his true identity and left
- Curse weakened Asuras for millennia
- Later Asuras begged for forgiveness
