## Characteristics

Krittika represents the power to burn, purify, and transform. It's associated with sharp cutting energy and the ability to separate truth from illusion.

### Positive Traits
- Sharp intellect and critical thinking
- Strong willpower and determination
- Protective and nurturing (despite the fierce energy)
- Honest and straightforward
- Good at cutting through problems
- Natural leaders
- Purifying influence on others

### Negative Traits
- Can be overly critical
- Sharp tongue, harsh speech
- May burn bridges
- Aggressive when threatened
- Impatient with incompetence
- Can be stubborn

## Special Notes

Krittika is unique as it spans two signs (Aries and Taurus), giving it a dual nature. It was the birth nakshatra of Lord Kartikeya (Murugan), the commander of celestial armies. This makes it especially powerful for protection and victory over enemies.
