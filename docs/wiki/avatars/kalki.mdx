# Kalki Avatar (The Future Warrior) ⚔️

## Overview

- **Form**: Future warrior on a white horse
- **Purpose**: Prophesied to end the current age of darkness and corruption, restoring righteousness

## Significance

Kalki represents the future, transformation, and the ultimate restoration of <span className="hindi">धर्म</span> (Dharma). Yet to appear, this avatar will mark the end of the current age.
