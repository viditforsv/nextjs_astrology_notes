# Gayatri Mantra

The Gayatri Mantra is one of the most powerful and revered mantras in Vedic tradition.

---

## Overview

The Gayatri Mantra is a sacred verse from the Rig Veda, dedicated to Savitr, the sun deity.

## Benefits

- Removes all kinds of pain
- Improved digestion
- Helps to absorb knowledge

## Practice

- Practice with <span className="hindi">श्रद्धा</span> (Shraddha - faith/devotion)
- Should be done with light or empty stomach
- Listening is equally beneficial as chanting, provided that your attention is there
