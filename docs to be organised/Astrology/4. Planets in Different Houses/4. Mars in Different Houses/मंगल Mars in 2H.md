1. **Anger & Speech**

   * Mars in 2nd shifts anger into speech.
   * Person becomes vocal, harsh, and can hurt others with words.
   * Under affliction, speech becomes extremely cutting.

2. **Oratory & Opposition**

   * Strong oratory power, often speaking *against* or in opposition.
   * Mars being planet of conflict makes their voice impactful in debates.

3. **Aspect on 5th House – Speculation**

   * Brings speculative tendency: stock market, lottery, gambling.
   * Creates strong involvement in risk-based ventures.

4. **Effect on Children**

   * Indicates accident, miscarriage, or difficulty with at least one child.
   * In female charts → miscarriage/abortion common.
   * If exalted but afflicted, danger to child may still persist.

5. **Family Conflicts**

   * Creates quarrels and rebellion within family.
   * Person may go against lineage or family traditions.

6. **Wealth & Income Sources**

   * Wealth linked with Mars-related fields: metals, energy, electricity, accountancy, electronics, spices.
   * If afflicted, wealth comes with struggles or disputes.

7. **Property Connection**

   * Mars aspects 8th house (ancestral property).
	   * 2nd H is 11th from 4th. 
   * Can give inheritance or property gains.

8. **Ambition & Drive**

   * Increases ambition, laboriousness, and winning attitude.
   * Malefic nature intensifies desire for achievements.

9. **Relation with Father**

   * Mars’ 8th aspect falls on 9th house → conflicts with father.
   * If Sun or 9th lord afflicted → early loss of father possible.

10. **Afflictions & Bad Combinations**

    * Mars + Saturn = highly dangerous combination.
    * Mars + Rahu also considered very bad, bringing violence or wrong paths.
	    * Rahu is like shani

11. **Eyes & Appearance**

    * Can affect eyesight → tendency for spectacles.
    * Face may show redness due to Mars’ fiery influence.

12. **Food Habits**

    * Preference for hot, spicy, bitter food.
    * With Rahu → attraction to non-veg.
    * With Sun → hot and spicy food.
    * With Moon → cold/soothing food.
    * With Jupiter → sweets.
    * Afflicted Mars worsens food habits.
    * Mars dont eat junk

13. **Advice**

    * Control food habits, avoid excess spice and heat.
    * Otherwise it can damage health over time.
