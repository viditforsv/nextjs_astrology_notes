### Uccha (Exalted) - ‡§â‡§ö‡•ç‡§ö

**Meaning:**

- Happy and comfortable

- Jo shakti ke charam pe hota hai wo shaant hota hai (One at peak of power is peaceful)

### Neecha (Debilitated) - ‡§®‡•Ä‡§ö

**Meaning:**

## Special Notes

### Rashi Digpal (Owner of Rashi)
- The lord of the rashi where planet sits

### Param Uchcha vs Param Neecha
- **Param Uchcha**: Maximum exaltation degree (most powerful)
- **Param Neecha**: Maximum debilitation (weakest point)
- Param neech uth bhi nahi paata (Cannot rise at all) - very negative results

### High Dignity in Difficult Houses
- High dignity planets (own sign or exalted) are good **even in difficult houses**
- Example: Jupiter in Sagittarius in 8th = deep knowledge, spiritual transformation (not bad)
