# Hora Shastra

The Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra is the foundational text of Vedic Astrology (Jyotish), authored by Maharishi Parasara. This comprehensive work systematically presents the principles, techniques, and applications of astrological science.

## Key Components of Hora Shastra

### Planetary System
- **Navagrahas** (Nine Planets): Sun, Moon, Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn, Rahu, Ketu
- Planetary characteristics and significations
- Planetary relationships and aspects

### House System
- **Twelve Houses** (Bhavas) and their significations
- House lords and their influences
- House combinations and effects

### Dasha System
- Planetary periods and sub-periods
- Vimshottari Dasha (120-year cycle)
- Timing of events through Dasha analysis

### Yogas (Planetary Combinations)
- Raj Yoga (royal combinations)
- Dhana Yoga (wealth combinations)
- Vipareeta Raja Yoga (reverse royal combinations)
- Various other significant combinations

### Predictive Techniques
- Transit analysis (Gochara)
- Annual charts (Varshaphal)
- Muhurta (electional astrology)
- Remedial measures (Upayas)

## Connection to Yoga Sutras

*This section explores how the astrological principles and planetary influences described in Hora Shastra relate to the ethical and spiritual practices outlined in Patanjali's Yoga Sutras.*

The planetary influences described by Maharishi Parasara work in harmony with the spiritual disciplines outlined by Maharishi Patanjali. Understanding both systems provides a complete framework for personal development.
