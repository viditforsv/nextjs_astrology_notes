# ⭕ Shatabhisha Nakshatra

## Overview

- **Number**: 24th Nakshatra
- **Symbol**: Empty Circle, 100 Flowers, 100 Physicians
- **Deity**: Varuna (God of Cosmic Waters and Sky)
- **Ruling Planet**: Rahu
- **Zodiac Sign**: Aquarius (6°40' - 20°00')
- **Element**: Ether
- **Guna**: Tamas (Inertia)
- **Nature**: Movable (Chara)
- **Caste**: Butcher
- **Gender**: Neuter
- **Body Part**: Right thigh, chin, cheeks

## Characteristics

Shatabhisha represents healing, mystery, and the power to cure. It's associated with hidden knowledge, research, and alternative healing.

### Positive Traits
- Healing abilities
- Research-oriented
- Independent and original
- Truth-seekers
- Mystical and intuitive
- Problem-solving skills
- Scientific mindset
- Protective of others

### Negative Traits
- Can be secretive and isolated
- May be harsh or blunt
- Difficulty trusting others
- Can be cynical
- May have health issues
- Tendency toward depression or melancholy

## Career Paths

People with strong Shatabhisha influence excel in:
- Medicine and healing (especially alternative)
- Research and investigation
- Astronomy and astrology
- Pharmaceuticals and chemistry
- Psychology and psychiatry
- Occult sciences
- Aviation and aerospace
- Electrical engineering
- Technology and innovation

## Special Notes

Shatabhisha is excellent for:
- Medical treatments and surgeries
- Research and experimentation
- Learning occult sciences
- Technological innovation
- Problem-solving
- Detoxification and healing

The empty circle represents:
- The void from which all emerges
- Infinite possibilities
- The mystery of existence
- Space and ether element

The 100 physicians or 100 stars represent:
- Multitude of healing methods
- Various approaches to truth
- Constellation in the night sky

**Special Characteristics**:
- Known as the "Veiling Star" or "Hundred Healers"
- Strongly connected to water, rain, and healing
- Gives interest in mysteries, research, and hidden knowledge
- Can indicate challenges that lead to deep healing

People born under Shatabhisha are often:
- Loners who value independence
- Gifted healers or researchers
- Interested in unconventional subjects
- Able to see what others cannot
- Natural problem-solvers

**Health Note**: This nakshatra can indicate health challenges, especially related to the circulatory system, but also gives strong healing abilities. Many doctors and healers are born here.

This is a powerful nakshatra for transformation through understanding the hidden causes of problems and applying appropriate remedies.
