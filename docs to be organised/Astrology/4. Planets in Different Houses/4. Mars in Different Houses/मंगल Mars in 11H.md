1. **Fulfillment of Desires**

   * 11th house = wishes, gains, ambitions.
   * Mars here makes desires fulfilled quickly.
   * Person wants results immediately, less focus on long-term vision.

2. **Financial Matters**

   * Very calculative and sharp in money-related decisions.
   * Can take big risks in financial matters.
   * Generally wealthy, courageous, and materially successful.

3. **Aspects**

   * Looks at 2nd house (wealth, family) → strong drive for financial gains.
   * Looks at 5th house (speculation, children) → increases risk-taking and impatience.
   * Looks at 6th house (enemies, debts) → gives power to defeat enemies easily.

4. **Materialistic Tendencies**

   * Strong placement for materialism and ambition.
   * Brings success in government jobs or high positions where influence matters.

5. **Corruption Indication**

   * Frequently seen in charts of corrupt officials.
   * They may take bribes or misuse power but rarely get caught.
   * Gives cleverness to escape legal trouble.

6. **Anger & Challenge**

   * Being “6th from the 6th” (*bhavat bhavam*), Mars here brings anger.
   * Anger is the main weakness of this placement.
   * If the person controls anger, they can win everything in life.
