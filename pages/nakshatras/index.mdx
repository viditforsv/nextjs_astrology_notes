## Importance of Nakshatras

### Moon Nakshatra (Janma Nakshatra)
The nakshatra occupied by the Moon at birth determines:
- Basic nature and temperament
- Vimshottari Dasha starting point
- Naming letters for the child
- Compatibility for marriage (Nakshatra matching)

### Applications
1. **Muhurta**: Selecting auspicious timings
2. **Compatibility**: Matching for marriage (Kuta system)
3. **Dashas**: Calculating planetary periods
4. **Remedies**: Specific remedies based on nakshatra
5. **Transit Analysis**: Understanding planetary movements

## Nakshatra Padas
Each nakshatra is divided into 4 padas (quarters) of 3Â°20' each:
- Pada 1: Corresponds to fire signs (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius)
- Pada 2: Corresponds to earth signs (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn)
- Pada 3: Corresponds to air signs (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius)
- Pada 4: Corresponds to water signs (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces)

## Nakshatra Groups

### By Quality (Guna)
- **Sattva**: Ashwini, Mrigashira, Punarvasu, Pushya, Hasta, Swati, Anuradha, Shravana, Revati
- **Rajas**: Bharani, Ardra, Magha, Purva Phalguni, Purva Ashadha, Purva Bhadrapada
- **Tamas**: Krittika, Ashlesha, Uttara Phalguni, Chitra, Vishakha, Jyeshtha, Mula, Uttara Ashadha, Dhanishtha, Shatabhisha, Uttara Bhadrapada

### By Nature
- **Cruel (Ugra/Tikshna)**: Bharani, Magha, Purva Phalguni, Purva Ashadha, Purva Bhadrapada
- **Mixed (Mishra)**: Krittika, Vishakha
- **Sharp (Tikshna)**: Ardra, Ashlesha, Jyeshtha, Mula
- **Soft (Mridu)**: Mrigashira, Chitra, Anuradha, Revati
- **Light (Laghu)**: Ashwini, Pushya, Hasta
- **Fixed (Sthira)**: Rohini, Uttara Phalguni, Uttara Ashadha, Uttara Bhadrapada
