1. **Marriage & Manglik Dosha**

   * 7th house = marriage, spouse, partnerships.
   * Mars here creates a **strong Manglik dosha**.
   * Causes conflicts and even violence in marriage if not exalted or well-placed.
   * The spouse’s longevity or well-being may be affected.

2. **Nature of Marriage Problems**

   * Saturn in 7th brings scarcity/sadness; Mars in 7th brings **conflict and violence**.
   * Some form of quarrel, aggression, or harshness in marriage is almost certain.

3. **Aspects**

   * Looks at **Lagna** → makes person disciplined, energetic, and attractive, but also desire-driven.
   * Looks at **2nd house (family)** → conflicts in family after spouse enters life. Can cause separation from family lineage.
   * Looks at **10th house (career/father)** → brings discipline in work, ambition in career, but can harm father’s longevity (since 10th is maraka for father).

4. **Impact on Spouse & Partnerships**

   * Spouse/partner tends to be aggressive or dominating.
   * If aspected by benefic planets → partnerships (marriage or business) give gains.
   * If with malefics → marital conflicts, separation, or violent breakups.

5. **Sexual Desires**

   * 7th house is Venus’s domain (marriage, passion).
   * Mars in 7th increases **sexual desire and intensity**.
   * Mars + Venus together (unless exalted) creates strong sensual inclinations.

6. **Business & Ambition**

   * 7th house also rules business and partnerships.
   * Mars here makes person very **ambitious in trade/enterprise**.
   * Gives success in Mars-related professions (engineering, technical fields, security, etc.) when linked with 10th house.
   * Brings discipline and aggression into work → often leads to high achievement.
   * Business with females because drishti on 2H

7. **Energy Matching in Partnerships**

   * Important to match Manglik energies even in business partnerships.
   * If one partner has strong Mars and the other is passive, conflicts arise.
   * Harmonious partnerships require similar energy levels.
