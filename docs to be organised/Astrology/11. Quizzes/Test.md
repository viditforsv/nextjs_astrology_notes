# Q3:  Vedas and Karmas
1. 4 Vedas
	1. Rig : talks about all the principles that exist in the world.
	2. Yajur :  talks about the application of principles
	3. Sama : talks about all the possible rhythms present in the universe.
	4. Atharva: talks about how everything impacts everything else.
2. 4 Karmas
	1. Kriyaman
		1. That we are doing right now. For eg: I am writing this test. 
	2. Agami
		1. That becomes our habit due to repeated actions. 
		2. eg: I like to eat certain types of food based on what I eat generally. I like certain activities based on my previous experiences of engaging with them. 
	3. Prarabdh : I am both in a certain caste, with a certain gender at a certain place. That is because of Prarabdh karma. Due to this I will lead a certain kind of lifestyle and move in some direction. But my kriyaman and agami can lead me to the direction in which I want to go. 
	4. Sanchit : Accumulated past karma from previous births
		1. Due to my past life karmas, I have accumulated good and bad. Total collection of all the past life karmas is sanchit.
