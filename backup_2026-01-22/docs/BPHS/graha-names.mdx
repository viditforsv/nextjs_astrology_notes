# Planet Names in BPHS Chapter 10

This document lists all the different names used for planets in Chapter 10 (अथारिष्टाध्यायः) of Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra.

---

## Sun (<span className="hindi">सूर्य</span>)

- **<span className="hindi">सूर्य</span>** (Surya) - most common name
- **<span className="hindi">अर्की</span>** (Arki) - Verse 5
- **<span className="hindi">अर्क</span>** (Arka) - Verse 6
- **<span className="hindi">रवि</span>** (Ravi) - Verse 27
- **<span className="hindi">भानु</span>** (Bhanu) - Verse 43
- **<span className="hindi">भास्कर</span>** (Bhaskara) - mentioned as <span className="hindi">भास्करपुत्रः</span> (Bhaskaraputra) for Saturn in Verse 9

---

## Moon (<span className="hindi">चंद्र</span>)

- **<span className="hindi">चंद्र/चन्द्र</span>** (Chandra) - most common name
- **<span className="hindi">इन्दु</span>** (Indu) - Verse 5
- **<span className="hindi">शशी</span>** (Shashi) - Verse 12
- **<span className="hindi">कलानिधि</span>** (Kalanidhi) - Verse 8
- **<span className="hindi">चन्द्रमा</span>** (Chandrama) - Verses 9, 24, 34

---

## Mars (<span className="hindi">मंगल</span>)

- **<span className="hindi">कुज</span>** (Kuja) - Verses 5, 39
- **<span className="hindi">भौम</span>** (Bhauma) - Verses 6, 7, 8, 34, 38
- **<span className="hindi">क्षितिज</span>** (Kshitija) - Verse 8
- **<span className="hindi">भूमनन्दन</span>** (Bhūmanandana) - Verse 37

---

## Mercury (<span className="hindi">बुध</span>)

- **<span className="hindi">बुध</span>** (Budha) - Verses 25, 35

---

## Jupiter (<span className="hindi">गुरु</span>)

- **<span className="hindi">गुरु</span>** (Guru) - Verse 10
- **<span className="hindi">जीव</span>** (Jiva) - Verses 9, 35, 42

---

## Venus (<span className="hindi">शुक्र</span>)

- **<span className="hindi">शुक्र</span>** (Shukra) - Verse 25
- **<span className="hindi">सुक्र</span>** (Sukra) - Verse 10

---

## Saturn (<span className="hindi">शनि</span>)

- **<span className="hindi">शनैश्चर</span>** (Shanaishchara) - Verses 10, 21, 39
- **<span className="hindi">सौरि</span>** (Sauri) - Verses 7, 8, 25
- **<span className="hindi">मन्द</span>** (Manda) - Verse 33 (as <span className="hindi">मन्दारौ</span> - Saturn and Mars together)
- **<span className="hindi">सन्द</span>** (Sanda) - Verse 34
- **<span className="hindi">भास्करपुत्र</span>** (Bhaskaraputra) - Verse 9 (lit. "son of the Sun")

---

## Rahu (<span className="hindi">राहु</span>)

- **<span className="hindi">राहु</span>** (Rahu) - Verses 7, 25, 37, 42

---

## Ketu (<span className="hindi">केतु</span>)

- Not mentioned in Chapter 10

---

**Note:** Some words like <span className="hindi">जीवति</span> (lives/survives) are verbs, not planet names, so they are excluded from this list.
