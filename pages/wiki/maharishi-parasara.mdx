# Maharishi Parasara

Maharishi Parasara is one of the most revered sages in Vedic tradition and is best known for his pioneering works on Vedic Astrology. He took mankind leaps ahead in evolution with his profound astrological insights.

## His Major Work

### Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra
The foundational text of Vedic Astrology, which systematically presents the principles and techniques of Jyotish (Vedic Astrology). This text forms the basis of astrological practice even today.

## Key Contributions

- Comprehensive system of planetary influences
- House significations and interpretations
- Dasha (planetary periods) systems
- Yogas (planetary combinations) and their effects
- Predictive techniques and remedial measures

## Legacy

Maharishi Parasara's teachings continue to be the cornerstone of Vedic Astrology, guiding astrologers and seekers worldwide in understanding the cosmic influences on human life.

His work represents a complete science of understanding how celestial bodies influence human consciousness and events, providing both predictive insights and remedial measures.
