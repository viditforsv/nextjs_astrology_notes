* **2nd house** = family, money, wealth, values.

* **4th house** = property, happiness, comforts, mother, mind.

* When the **4th lord goes to the 2nd house**:

  * Property lord joins money house → excellent for **earning through property**.
  * 4L in 2H is **11th from itself**, so it gives **wish fulfillment**.
  * Native seeks happiness in **money and family life**.
  * Brings **pleasure, wealth, family happiness, and honor**.
  * Person may also be adventurous.

* **Affliction case:**

  * If afflicted, the person can become **cunning and deceptive**.
  * Why? Because Venus (innocence) and Moon (emotion) influence family values in 2H.
  * If afflicted by malefics (Rahu, Ketu, etc.), the innocence and softness of family values are lost → person may **use emotions negatively** for selfish ends.
  * May **do bad deeds or manipulations for money**.

* **If unafflicted:**

  * Very auspicious: brings **immense money, family happiness, fulfillment of desires**.
  * Can give wealth like **Ashta Lakshmi** (all kinds of prosperity).

* **Even if afflicted:**

  * Person can still be rich, but often through **wrong means or cheating others**.

---

✅ **In short:**

* **4th lord in 2nd house** is a powerful wealth-giving placement.
* **Good/benefic** → wealth through property, happy family life, desires fulfilled, Ashta Lakshmi blessings.
* **Afflicted** → riches may still come, but through cunning, deception, or unethical ways.
