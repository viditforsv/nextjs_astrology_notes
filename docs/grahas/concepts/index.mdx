# Planetary Concepts

Concepts and principles that apply to all planets in Vedic Astrology.

## Planetary States & Strengths

### [Planetary States (Avasthas)](/grahas/concepts/planetary-states)
Exaltation, debilitation, Moolatrikona, and other states that affect planetary strength.

### [Digbala (Directional Strength)](/grahas/concepts/digbal)
Directional strength that planets gain when placed in specific houses.

### [Retrograde & Combustion](/grahas/concepts/retrograde-combustion)
Special conditions: retrograde motion and combustion (being too close to the Sun).

## Planetary Relationships & Interactions

### [Drishti (Aspects)](/grahas/concepts/drishti)
How planets aspect each other and the houses they influence.

### [Maitreyi (Friendships)](/grahas/concepts/maitreyi)
Planetary friendships and enmities - which planets are natural friends or enemies.

### [Maran Karaka Sthan](/grahas/concepts/maran-karaka-sthan)
Death-inflicting places - houses where planets can cause harm.
