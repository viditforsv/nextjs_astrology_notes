
## Jupiter in 11th House (Labh Sthana)

### General Significance

* 11th house = *Labh Sthana* (gains, fulfilment of desires, networks, elder siblings).
* Jupiter here:

  * Brings **materialistic gains** after key life events (e.g., birth of children).
  * Gives **good, wise, and supportive friends**.
  * Friends are fewer but of very high quality; excellent company and moral support.

### Relationship & Marriage Impact

* If Jupiter is in 11th from Lagna, it is **5th from the 7th house** (Pancham Bhava from spouse):

  * Spouse’s social circle is large and influential.
  * Spouse has many friends; native’s own friends are fewer but very close.
* Jupiter in 11th aspects the **7th house (marriage)**:

  * Can **save and sustain marriage** even under stress.
  * Brings dharmic (righteous) approach to relationships.

### Additional Points

* If in a woman’s chart, 9th house Jupiter:

  * She may consider her husband as a father-like figure.
* Jupiter’s **Dharm Drishti** on 7th house ensures fulfillment of marital duties.
