# Sun (Surya)

## Basic Information

- **Sanskrit Name**: Surya, Ravi
- **Nature**: Masculine, Hot, Dry
- **Element**: Fire (Agni)
- **Guna**: Sattva (with some Rajas)
- **Caste**: Kshatriya (Warrior)
- **Direction**: East

## Significations

### General
- Soul (Atma)
- Father
- Authority and Government
- Leadership
- Vitality and Health
- Confidence and Self-esteem

### Body Parts
- Heart
- Right eye (for males)
- Bones
- Stomach

### Profession
- Government officials
- Politicians
- Administrators
- Medical doctors
- Leaders and CEOs

- **Own Sign**: Leo

For each ascendant, Sun rules the 5th house from Leo.

## Dasha Period
6 years in Vimshottari Dasha

## Gemstone

## Day
Sunday (Ravivar)

## Tips for Strengthening Sun
- Wake up early and watch sunrise
- Offer water to Sun at sunrise
- Practice Surya Namaskar (Sun Salutation)
- Wear ruby gemstone (after consultation)
- Donate wheat, jaggery, or copper on Sundays
