- Ladai in ghar
1. **General Nature**

   * 4th house = happiness, mother, homeland, property.
   * In *Kalpurush Kundli*, it is Cancer (Moon’s sign), where Mars is debilitated.
   * Hence, Mars here is generally **inauspicious** unless exalted or in own sign.

2. **Effect on Happiness & Character**

   * Creates inner heat (tāpa), reducing domestic peace and pleasure.
   * If afflicted (by Rahu, Ketu, Saturn, etc.), may bring issues in character and reputation.
   * Person may sacrifice personal character in pursuit of pleasure.

3. **Mother & Homeland**

   * Strained relationship with mother, conflicts, disagreements.
   * Often takes the person away from homeland or motherland; tendency to live abroad or away from home.

4. **Marriage & Spouse**

   * Mars aspects 7th house from here, creating a **Manglik combination**.
   * Spouse’s longevity not necessarily threatened, but **marital happiness is reduced**.
   * The expected joy or harmony from marriage is lessened.

5. **Career & Profession**

   * Mars aspects 10th house → person works in Mars-related fields (engineering, technical fields, property-related work).
   * Can bring success in career if supported by other good combinations.

6. **Property & Assets**

   * Mars is significator of land/property.
   * Person tends to acquire self-earned property, often in the form of land/house rather than flats.
   * Strong potential for wealth through immovable assets.
