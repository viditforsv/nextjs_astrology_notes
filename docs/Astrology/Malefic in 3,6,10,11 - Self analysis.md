
# Theory
1. Koi planet hamesha shubh ya ashubh nahi hota
2. ⁠Similarly, koi bhav hamesha shubh ya ashubh nahi hota. 
3. ⁠Malefic in 3,6,10, 11 main acche hain is a general statement - dont apply this with a blindfold. Kuch specific context ke liye true hai. Kuch ke liye false hai. Kuch ke liye neutral hai. 
4. ⁠Factors to influence this
	1. Kis rashi main
	2. Kis ko dekhta hai, kisse dekha jaata hai 
	3. Shadbala
	4. Bhavat bhavam se kiske liye accha hai kiske liye nahi hai. 

# 3H
Courage, Initiative, parakram dega. 
- **Shani** → steady parakram (late bloom).
- **Mangal** → direct parakram (quick action, aggression).
- **Rahu** → unconventional parakram (risk-taking, manipulative).
- **Ketu** → detached parakram (fearless but confused).
# 6H
Shatru pe vijay hone ka bal dega

- **Shani** → Discipline, duty, service orientation. Enemies ko dheere-dheere thakakar harata hai. Diseases chronic banata hai, par insaan ko dhairya aur seva bhaav deta hai. Long and disciplined battles main jeet deta hai
- **Mangal** → Direct fighter. Enemies pe attack karke jeet dilata hai. Aggressive discipline. Rog aur chot bhi de sakta hai, par jeet bhi dilata hai. Saamne se maarta hai
- **Rahu** → Chalaki se enemies pe jeet. Legal, political, competitive settings mein advantage. Diseases strange/hidden ho sakte hain. Debt-related risks.  Dhoke se maarta hai
- **Ketu** → Strange courage against enemies. Rog/ enemies ko cut karke alag kar deta hai, par kabhi kabhi andar se weak health/detachment. Spiritual service bhi laa sakta hai. Shatru ko pata hi nahi chalta ki kahan se attack aaya
# 10H
Kaam aur siddhi prapt karne ka bal dega
- **Shani** → Discipline, duty, responsibility. Long-term struggle → late but stable career growth. Authority through patience. Permanent growth in work. 
- **Mangal** → Bold initiatives, leadership, competitive spirit in profession. Direct action se career banaata hai, par impulsiveness se conflicts bhi. Digbal bhi hai.
- **Rahu** → Unconventional career path. Fame, foreign connections, shortcuts in rajya. Public image ko magnify karta hai (good/bad dono).
- **Ketu** → Detachment from authority. Spiritual/dharmic professions ya technical detail-oriented karma. Public life mein low profile, par andar se mastery. Digbal bhi hai
# 11H
- Iccha purti ke liye sangharsh karne ka bal dega. 

- **Shani** → Steady gains through discipline & patience. Network stable but slow to build. Elder siblings se zimmedari/strain. Late but long-lasting fulfillment of desires.
- **Mangal** → Quick gains, bold networking, competitive edge. Strong ambitions, par impulsive desires bhi. Elder siblings ke saath aggression possible.
- **Rahu** → Sudden, large, unconventional gains. Foreign contacts, influential networks. Desires unlimited, greed bhi ho sakti hai. Fame ya scandal dono.
- **Ketu** → Detached gains. Unexpected benefits, par apne goals mein udasi/aloofness. Elder siblings se distance.
