## Characteristics

Uttara Phalguni represents patronage, agreements, and the responsibilities that come after pleasure. It balances the indulgence of Purva Phalguni with duty.

### Positive Traits
- Helpful and supportive
- Reliable and dependable
- Good at forming partnerships
- Ethical and principled
- Kind and generous
- Leadership with responsibility
- Organized and methodical
- Strong sense of justice

### Negative Traits
- Can be overly serious
- May be rigid about rules
- Difficulty relaxing
- Can be judgmental
- May neglect own needs for others
- Stubborn in beliefs

## Special Notes

Uttara Phalguni is considered highly auspicious for:
- Marriage ceremonies
- Signing contracts and agreements
- Starting partnerships
- Property deals
- Social gatherings
- Forming alliances

This nakshatra bridges Leo (royalty) and Virgo (service), combining authority with humility. People born here often serve as mediators and peacemakers, helping others form lasting bonds and agreements.
