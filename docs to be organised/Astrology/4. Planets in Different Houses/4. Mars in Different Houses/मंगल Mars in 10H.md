1. **Exaltation & Digbala**

   * 10th house = Capricorn in Kalpurush chart, where Mars is **exalted**.
   * Mars also gains **Digbala** (directional strength) in 10th.
   * Considered the **best placement for Mars**.

2. **Work & Career**

   * Native becomes extremely ambitious, hardworking, disciplined.
   * Work is always completed on time and with perfection.
   * Career involves constant struggle, but Mars ensures eventual victory.
   * Such people rise to leadership in their profession.

3. **Focus & Dedication**

   * Mars in 10th aspects Lagna → gives courage, energy, and ambition.
   * Native derives happiness and satisfaction through work rather than emotions.
   * Emotions (4th house) are sacrificed for career; dedication to duty dominates.

4. **Professional Fields**

   * Excels in Mars-related directions: politics, police, army, security, electronics, engineering, technical professions.
   * Once they choose a field, they cannot be stopped → natural leaders.

5. **Negative Possibility**

   * If associated with other malefics, Mars in 10th can make the native cruel, autocratic, and harsh in workplace.
   * Can treat others like subordinates/slaves.

6. **Overall Outcome**

   * One of the strongest placements for Mars.
   * Gives ambition, discipline, courage, and leadership.
   * Ensures success in career through perseverance and strength.
