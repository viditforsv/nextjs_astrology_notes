# Planetary Karakatva

Each planet is a natural significator (karaka) for specific aspects of life in Vedic astrology. Understanding planetary karakatva helps in analyzing various life areas.

## Planetary Significations

### <span className="hindi">सूर्य</span> (Sun)
- Self, personality, physical body
- Father, authority, ego
- Soul, vitality, life force
- Government, administration
- Leadership, power

### <span className="hindi">चंद्र</span> (Moon)
- Mind, emotions, feelings
- Mother, public, masses
- Home, domestic life
- Water, liquids
- Travel, movement

### <span className="hindi">मंगल</span> (Mars)
- Energy, courage, valor
- Siblings (especially younger)
- Land, property
- Enemies, conflicts
- Accidents, injuries
- Service, employment

### <span className="hindi">बुध</span> (Mercury)
- Intelligence, communication
- Education, learning
- Business, trade
- Speech, writing
- Maternal uncles
- Mathematics, calculations

### <span className="hindi">गुरु</span> (Jupiter)
- Wisdom, knowledge, teachers
- Children, progeny
- Wealth, prosperity
- Husband (for females)
- Religion, spirituality
- Higher learning, philosophy

### <span className="hindi">शुक्र</span> (Venus)
- Spouse, marriage, partnerships
- Relationships, love
- Luxury, comforts
- Arts, beauty, aesthetics
- Vehicles, conveyances
- Entertainment, pleasures

### <span className="hindi">शनि</span> (Saturn)
- Longevity, life span
- Delays, obstacles
- Discipline, hard work
- Servants, subordinates
- Old age, chronic diseases
- Losses, expenses
- Spirituality, detachment

### <span className="hindi">राहु</span> (Rahu)
- Desires, ambitions
- Foreign lands, foreign connections
- Illusions, deceptions
- Material pursuits
- Technology, modern things
- Addictions, vices

### <span className="hindi">केतु</span> (Ketu)
- Spirituality, moksha
- Detachment, renunciation
- Past life karma
- Mysticism, occult
- Liberation, enlightenment
- Losses, separation
