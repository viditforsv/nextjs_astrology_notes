# Maitreyi (मैत्रेयी) - Planetary Friendships

## Overview

<span className="hindi">मैत्रेयी</span> (Maitreyi) refers to the friendship and enmity relationships between planets in Vedic Astrology. These relationships determine how planets interact with each other and influence their strength when placed in each other's signs.

## Three Types of Relationships

1. **Mitra (मित्र)** - Friend
2. **Shatru (शत्रु)** - Enemy  
3. **Sama (सम)** - Neutral

## Effects of Friendship

### Planet in Friend's Sign
- Planet gains strength and support
- Positive results are enhanced
- Planet feels comfortable and supported
- Works harmoniously with the sign lord

### Planet in Enemy's Sign
- Planet loses strength
- Negative traits may be amplified
- Planet feels uncomfortable
- May create friction or conflict
- Results may be delayed or problematic

### Planet in Neutral Sign
- Planet functions normally
- Neither particularly strong nor weak
- Neutral influence on results

## Important Notes

- Friendship relationships affect the **strength** of a planet, not just its nature
- A planet in friend's sign is like being in a supportive environment
- A planet in enemy's sign is like being in a hostile environment
- These relationships are considered along with exaltation, debilitation, and own sign
- Conjunction with friendly planets enhances results
- Conjunction with enemy planets may create conflicts

## Practical Application

When analyzing a chart:
1. Check which sign the planet is placed in
2. Identify the lord of that sign
3. Determine if the planet and sign lord are friends, enemies, or neutral
4. This adds another layer to understanding planetary strength and results

## Example

If <span className="hindi">गुरु</span> (Jupiter) is placed in <span className="hindi">मेष</span> (Aries):
- Lord of Aries is <span className="hindi">मंगल</span> (Mars)
- <span className="hindi">गुरु</span> and <span className="hindi">मंगल</span> are **friends**
- Therefore, <span className="hindi">गुरु</span> gains strength from being in friend's sign
- Results will be more positive and supportive
