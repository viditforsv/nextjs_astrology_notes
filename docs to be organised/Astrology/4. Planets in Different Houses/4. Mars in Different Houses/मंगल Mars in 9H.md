1. **Father & Relationship**

   * 9th house = father, dharma, fortune (*bhagya*).
   * Mars here generally creates problems with father; relation becomes strained.
   * If not exalted, in own sign, or blessed by Jupiter → can reduce father’s longevity.

2. **Religious Views**

   * People with Mars in 9th are often **rebellious about religion**.
   * Tend to be dogmatic, orthodox, or rigid in beliefs.
   * May reject temple visits and rituals, questioning their meaning.
   * Can even show intolerance towards other faiths if Mars is afflicted.

3. **Destiny & Fortune (Bhagya)**

   * Mars in 9th blocks smooth fortune.
   * Native must struggle for everything in life; nothing comes easily.
   * Often drags the person into conflicts, disputes, or court cases.
   * If lawyer combinations exist, they may enter law profession; otherwise, life becomes a series of legal/argumentative struggles.

4. **Past Life Karma**

   * Placement reflects misuse of power and violence in past births.
   * In this life, results in obstacles in fortune and constant fights.

5. **Aspects**

   * Looks at **12th house** → expenses on conflicts, courts, hospitals.
   * Looks at **3rd house (siblings)** → problems with younger siblings, sometimes denial of siblings altogether.

     * If Jupiter aspects 3rd → siblings exist, but still conflicts remain.
   * Creates rebelliousness even towards teachers and gurus.
