### 6th Lord in 6th House — Notes

* **12th Moon** → acts opposite to advice, self-willed, hard to guide; must be challenged to act.
* **Rahu in 5th** → razor-sharp intellect, scientific, analytical, obsessive thinker.

**Main Concept:**

* 6th lord in 6th strengthens the house → life full of conflicts, service, struggle.
* Brings constant fights and inner unrest but strong ability to endure and win.
* Person is a **fighter**, wins through persistence.

**If in own sign (swarāśi):**

* Native gains power over enemies and disease.
* Maternal family (nanihaal) is supportive or wealthy.
* Relationship linked to that planet helps the native.

**If afflicted:**

* Endless conflicts, restlessness, reduced happiness.

**If benefic influence (esp. Jupiter):**

* Enemies become teachers; competition uplifts rather than harms.
* Victory comes through calmness, not reaction.
* Staying silent invites Jupiter’s protection; reacting loses it.

**Combust/exalted:**

* Burns impurities, gives controlled, purposeful energy.

**Mercury logic:**

* 6th is Mercury’s natural domain (Kaal Purusha); gives strategy and intellect.
* For Cancer Lagna, Jupiter rules the 6th → ethical, wisdom-based fighting spirit.

**Core idea:**

* Continuous struggle + immense resilience.
* Win through patience, wisdom, and self-control.
