Gulika is the severed leg of Saturn. Legend says Ravana tried to force all planets into the lucky 11th house for his son's birth. Saturn, being rebellious, stretched his leg into the unlucky 12th house. Ravana cut it off, and that piece became Gulika.

Toxic: He poisons whatever house he sits in (except 3, 6, 11).
