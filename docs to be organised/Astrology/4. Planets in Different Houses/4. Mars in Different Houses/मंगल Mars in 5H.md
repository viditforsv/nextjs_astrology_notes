1. **Children & Health**

   * Problems with children: denial, miscarriage, accidents, or difficulties with offspring.
   * Creates stomach issues, possibility of surgery.
   * In female charts, can indicate ovarian or uterine operations.

2. **Courage & Temperament**

   * Makes the person courageous, bold, warrior-like.
   * In female charts, gives strong masculine traits and inner strength.
   * Heightens aggression and fiery temperament.

3. **Speculation & Risk**

   * 5th house governs speculation; Mars here gives strong gambling, share market, lottery tendencies.
   * Native may lose money due to excessive risk-taking.
   * Mars is the biggest risk-taker among planets; if well placed, risks succeed, if ill-placed, cause heavy losses.

4. **Education & Intellect**

   * 5th house = education, intellect.
   * Mars here makes intellect technical, regardless of formal education stream.
   * Inclination toward experiments, fixing instruments, practical problem-solving.

5. **Karmic Indications**

   * Placement reflects violent deeds from past births.
   * Those karmas manifest in this life as accidents, conflicts, or struggles.

6. **Elemental & Temperamental Effect**

   * 5th house in Kalpurush = Leo (Sun’s sign, fiery).
   * Mars, also fiery, increases mental heat and disturbs temperament.
   * Person becomes highly daring but also restless and quick-tempered.

7. **Overall Outcome**

   * Not considered a good placement for Mars unless exalted or in own/friendly sign.
   * Brings constant inner clashes and loss of peace of mind.
   * Remedies for malefic planets in Lagna, 5th, or 9th houses are essential for balance.
