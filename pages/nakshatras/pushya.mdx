## Characteristics

Pushya is considered one of the most auspicious nakshatras. It represents nourishment, growth, and spiritual wisdom.

### Positive Traits
- Nurturing and caring
- Wise and knowledgeable
- Calm and peaceful demeanor
- Good counselors and advisors
- Spiritual inclination
- Disciplined and organized
- Protective of loved ones
- Respected in society

### Negative Traits
- Can be overly protective
- Rigid and orthodox
- May be stubborn
- Sometimes too serious
- Difficulty in accepting change

## Special Notes

Pushya is so auspicious that it's considered good for almost all activities. It's especially powerful for:
- Spiritual initiations
- Starting new education
- Marriage ceremonies
- Purchasing property
- Beginning any important venture

Pushya Nakshatra occurs on Thursday (Pushya Guru Yoga) is considered extremely auspicious for all spiritual and material undertakings. Many important Hindu ceremonies prefer this nakshatra.
