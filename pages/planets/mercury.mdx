- **Sanskrit Name**: Budha
- **Nature**: Neutral (adapts to companions), Peaceful
- **Element**: Earth (Prithvi)
- **Direction**: North (prosperity direction)
- **Metal**: Bronze
- **Color**: Green
- **Gemstone**: Emerald (Panna)
- **Age**: 32 years
- **Day**: Wednesday (Budhvar)
- **Gender**: Neuter
- **Varna**: Vaishya (Merchant/Trader)
- **Deity**: Lord Vishnu

## Drishti (Aspect)
- **7th House** - Brings learning desire, childish curiosity

## Speed & Motion
- Retrograde period: 20-24 days every 4 months
- Always within ±1 rashi of Sun
- Can be retrograde and combust simultaneously

## Special Characteristics

- **Always active** - 24x7 (That's why money never sleeps)
- **Extremely powerful in Kaliyuga** (Age of information and money)
- **Adapts to companions**: Jiske saath baithta hai waisa ho jaata hai (Becomes like whoever it sits with)
- **Diplomat and Prince**
- Creates imbalance of all 3 doshas: Vatta, Pitta, and Kapha

## Mercury vs Jupiter

- **Mercury** = Learning, intellect, information, raw knowledge
- **Jupiter** = Wisdom, transformation of intellect into deeper knowledge
- One must move beyond intellect (Mercury) to attain wisdom (Jupiter)
- Mercury and Jupiter are father and son in mythology

## Astronomical Facts ☿

- **Position from Sun**: 1st planet (closest)
- **Distance from Sun**: ~57.9 million km (average)
- **Diameter**: 4,879 km (smallest planet in solar system)
- **Mass**: 3.3 × 10²³ kg (~5.5% of Earth)
- **Gravity**: 3.7 m/s² (~38% of Earth)
- **Orbital Period**: 88 Earth days (fastest planet)
- **Rotational Period**: 59 Earth days
- **Day Length** (sunrise to sunrise): 176 Earth days
- **Orbital Speed**: ~47.9 km/s (fastest planet)

### Temperature
- Daytime: Up to +430°C
- Nighttime: Down to -180°C
- No atmosphere to trap heat - extreme temperature swings

### Surface & Features
- Covered with craters (like Moon)
- Huge cliffs and ridges due to planetary contraction
- No moons or rings
- Large iron core contributing to weak magnetic field

### Exploration
- First Flyby: Mariner 10 (1974-75)
- Orbiter: MESSENGER (2004-2015)
- Upcoming: BepiColombo (ESA + JAXA, arriving 2025)

### Interesting Facts
- No atmosphere = no weather, no erosion
- Closest planet to Sun, but NOT the hottest (Venus is)
- Visible to naked eye but hard to spot (close to horizon)
- Tidally locked in 3:2 spin-orbit resonance
- A single day (sunrise to sunrise) is longer than its year!

## Retrograde Mercury

### Effects
- Super intelligent but introverted
- Over-analyzes everything
- May speak less but with precision
- Strong comic sense and common sense
- Known for reverse logic or unexpected insights
- Confused yet insightful
- Often misjudged in meetings but brilliant in hindsight

### Stammering Connection
- Stammering can be linked to retrograde Mercury
- Internal overthinking disconnects speech and brain
- Give confidence and expression - the block releases

## Combust Mercury

### Effects
- Frequent combustion due to closeness to Sun
- Logic and speech may be impaired
- Silent intellect
- Protected mind
- Speaks less

### Retrograde + Combust Mercury
- Highly intelligent and cultured
- Reserved and calculative
- Often misjudged but very refined
