# Chanting

Chanting is a spiritual practice involving the repetition of sacred texts and names.

## Overview

This section contains various chanting practices and texts.

## Available Chantings

- **[Vishnu Sahasranam](/chanting/vishnu-sahasranam)** - The thousand names of Lord Vishnu
- **[Gayatri Mantra](/chanting/gayatri-mantra)** - One of the most powerful mantras in Vedic tradition
- **[Lalitha Sahasranam](/chanting/lalitha-sahasranam)** - The thousand names of Goddess Lalitha Tripura Sundari
- **[Graha Beej Mantras](/chanting/graha-beej-mantras)** - Seed mantras for the nine planets
