# 3rd Bhava

## Basic Information

- **Sanskrit Name**: <span className="hindi">सहज भाव</span> (Siblings House)
- **Also Known As**: <span className="hindi">मैथुन भाव</span> (Maithun Bhava) - House of sex<br/>House of struggle and war<br/>House of short travel<br/>House of winning
- **Significator**: <span className="hindi">मंगल</span> (Mars)
- **Classification**: <span className="hindi">उपचय</span> (Upachaya/Growth House)
