# 8th Bhava

## Basic Information

- **Sanskrit Name**: <span className="hindi">आयु भाव</span> (Longevity House)
- **Also Known As**: Discharge Area<br/>Like a pit<br/>House of Age<br/>House of Lineage<br/>Also like a debt house, but only you are <span className="hindi">भागीदार</span> (Bhagidar)
- **Significator**: <span className="hindi">शनि</span> (Saturn)
- **Classification**: <span className="hindi">त्रिषडाय</span> (Trisadaya/Malefic)
- **Note**: Considered a negative <span className="hindi">स्थान</span> (sthan)<br/><span className="hindi">कुंडलिनी</span> (Kundalini) and <span className="hindi">मूलाधार चक्र</span> (Muladhar Chakra) here<br/><span className="hindi">प्राण</span> (Prana) in 8th house<br/>At one end it is very positive and also very negative<br/>There can be anything in this house since it is mysterious<br/><span className="hindi">मंगल</span> (Mangal) - Give accidents, <span className="hindi">केतु</span> (Ketu) - Brings fear here
