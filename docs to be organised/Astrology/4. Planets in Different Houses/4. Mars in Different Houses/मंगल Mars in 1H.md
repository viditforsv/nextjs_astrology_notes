
1. **Power of Mars in Lagna**

   * In *Kalpurush Kundli*, Lagna is Aries, which is Mars’ own *Mool Trikona* sign → hence very powerful.
   * Being a malefic, Mars gives both benefits and challenges.

2. **Nature of Energy**

   * Mars = fire (*agni*), different from Sun’s light (*jyoti*).
   * Brings **hyperactivity**, extreme energy, and drive to win.
   * Makes the native **self-centered**, focused on fulfilling desires.

3. **Effect of Aspects**

   * If benefic aspects present → energy and desire get balanced → strong focus, success.
   * If afflicted/alone → creates anger, restlessness, and over-desire.
   * Sits on *ajna chakra* → increases mental fire → hyperactivity + anger.

4. **Impact on Mind & Emotions**

   * If Moon is afflicted too → person becomes very easily angered.
   * Anger and fire in brain remain high → inner restlessness.

5. **Physical Indications**

   * Mars rules blood → face often reddish, glowing.
   * Marks, scars, or moles on face/forehead are common.
   * Old custom: such signs used to judge Manglik.

6. **Manglik Effect**

   * Mars in 1st makes person **Manglik**.
   * Concerns around spouse’s longevity due to Mars’ 7th and 8th aspect.
   * Deadliest aspect = 8th house drishti.
   * Must check both Lagna chart and Chandra chart for Manglik status.

7. **Wealth & Beauty**

   * Person may be wealthy, attractive, with radiant aura (reddish Mars glow).
   * Face shines even more in sunlight.

8. **Influence on Spouse**

   * Mars in Lagna = Mars also falls in spouse’s 7th (from their chart view).
   * Spouse may be short-tempered.
   * Needs confirmation from *Navamsha*.

9. **Health & Weakness**

   * Being malefic, Mars weakens Lagna → health issues occur.
   * Its aspect on 7th (maraka house) also adds challenges.
   * Such natives often keep internalized anger, burning within.
