### Fundamental Concepts

- **Activation:** The Navamsha chart is active from birth. It does not wait for marriage to activate; it represents the fruit of the tree (D1) and remains active throughout life.
- **Purpose:** While D1 (Birth Chart) shows how society views your marriage (e.g., public fighting or affection), the D9 reveals the **internal experience** of the marriage and the true nature of the relationship between the two souls.

## Astrologer's Ethics and Conduct

### Intent Matters

Astrology should be practiced with a pure intent to help, not to make money or sell gemstones. Cheating clients or being greedy destroys the "Vidya" (knowledge).

### Humility

An astrologer must remain grounded. Fame and talent are different things. Arrogance leads to a downfall.

### Counseling

Astrologers act like doctors for serious problems. Even if a prediction is negative, the client should leave with reassurance or understanding, not fear.

### Universal Remedy

The mantra "Om Namo Narayan" or "Om Namah Shivaya" is recommended for everyone as a complete remedy.
