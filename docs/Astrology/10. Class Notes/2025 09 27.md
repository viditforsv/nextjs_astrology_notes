# Neech Bhang Raj Yoga
1. Neechta ka bhang ho jae to wo ucch ke results ya raj yoga deta hai
2. Uccha ka matlab - you have all the resources
3. Neech matlab - that means us graha se related pratibha nahi di gayi. Neech bhang matlab - self learned skills
4. Neech ke graha ki pratibha purushartha se leni padegi. 
5. Neech bhang matlab support mil gaya.
6. Kalyug main zyadatar logon ke kundli main atleast 1 planet vipreet raj yoga ya neech bhang main hoga
### Conditions of Neech Bhang
1. Neech planet is in kendra from Janm or Chandra lagn
	- No outside support
2. Neech planet jis Rashi main hai, uska lord Janm or Chandra lagn main kendra main ho to
	- Mars in Kark Rashi and moon in kendra like 4H from either the janm lagn or chandra lagn. Chandra lagn se to hoga hi. 
	- Support from the very graha jo neech kar raha hai
3.  Jab, neech planet ki rashi main jo planet uccha hota hai wo kendra main chala jae to neech bhang ho jaata hai
	- eg: Mars in karka and jupiter is in kendra from either lagn
4.  Neech planet jis rashi main ho, us rashi ka lord apne ghar ko dekh le, to bhi neech bhang hota hai
	- for eg: mars in karka, and if moon aspects karka. 
	- eg: mercury is neech in meen, guru kahin se bhi meen rashi ko dekh le to neech bhang ho jaega
Neech bhang jab hota hai to pehle neech ke result aate hain, then bhang ke and then raj yoga milta hai.
