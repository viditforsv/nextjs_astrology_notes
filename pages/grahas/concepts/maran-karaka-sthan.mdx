# Maran Karaka Sthan

## Maran Karaka Sthan for Planets

<span className="hindi">मरण कारक स्थान</span> (Maran Karaka Sthan) refers to the houses where planets become death-inflicting or cause significant challenges.

| Planet | Maran Karaka Sthan | Remarks |
|--------|-------------------|---------|
| <span className="hindi">सूर्य</span> (Sun) | 12th house | 12th = Loss, <span className="hindi">बंधन</span> (Bandhan). Sun = <span className="hindi">राजा</span> (Raja). Problems in education, health, less success in native country. Example: Mahatma Gandhi. <span className="hindi">ऐसे लोग सरकार गिराते हैं</span> (Aise log sarkar girate hain). |
| <span className="hindi">चंद्र</span> (Moon) | 8th house | <span className="hindi">मन से काष्ठ</span> (Man se kashtha). <span className="hindi">बचपन में अरिष्ट होता है</span> (Bachpan mein arisht hota hai). <span className="hindi">उसकी वजह से माँ का भी अरिष्ट होता है</span> (Uski wajah se maa ka bhi arisht hota hai). Issues with water, mother, blood. Psychosomatic issues. Depression is obvious. Emotional torture. <span className="hindi">बाल अरिष्ट</span> (Bal Arisht). |
| <span className="hindi">गुरु</span> (Jupiter) | 3rd house | Guru doesn't like to work hard. He likes meditation. Doesn't like to create karma. 3rd house is house of initiative and struggle. Thus more karma created. |
| <span className="hindi">मंगल</span> (Mars) | 7th house | <span className="hindi">जीवन पूरा बदल जाएगा</span> (Jeevan pura badal jaega). Mars loves freedom. These people feel choked in marriage. Mars is violence. These people can face violence even if it is small. Even if <span className="hindi">उच्च</span> (Uccha) mangal, even then marriage would be a little struggle. May face violence. Exalted mars will not save. There is love in exalted and <span className="hindi">स्वराशि</span> (Swarashi) mars. |
| <span className="hindi">शुक्र</span> (Venus) | 6th house | Restrictions in relationship. Specially in physical relationship. Conflict in 1st marriage. Unable to perform. Problems in love life, restrictions in relationships. |
| <span className="hindi">शनि</span> (Saturn) | 1st house | <span className="hindi">व्यक्ति को अपने लिए शनि की feeling रहती है</span> (Vyakti ko apne liye shani ki feeling rehti hai). Shani se related things main marne jaisi feeling. Like - Timing, discipline, punctuality, Agility. Poor time management. Poor Initiative. Health Issues, less confidence. <span className="hindi">स्वराशि</span> (Swarashi) or <span className="hindi">उच्च</span> (Uccha) Shani. |
| <span className="hindi">बुध</span> (Mercury) | 4th house | According to some schools, person is mama's boy, learning and risk taking capacity is low. <span className="hindi">बच्चा डब्बो हो जाता है</span> (Baccha dabbo ho jaata hai). |
| <span className="hindi">बुध</span> (Mercury) | 7th house | <span className="hindi">बुध</span> (Buddha) - <span className="hindi">नपुंसक</span> (napunsak), 11-12 years age. Cannot perform. No capacity to perform. For female chart, husband cannot perform. Person is childish in marriage. Person is childish and immature. |
| <span className="hindi">राहु</span> (Rahu) | 9th house | <span className="hindi">अधर्मी</span> (Adharmi) in <span className="hindi">मंदिर</span> (Mandir). Rahu top pe lejakar girata hai. <span className="hindi">धर्म के विरुद्ध काम</span> (Dharm ke virrudh kaam), <span className="hindi">गुरु द्रोह</span> (guru droh). <span className="hindi">हाथ करके जीत जाएगा</span> (Hat karke jeet jaega) agar rahu pe <span className="hindi">शुभ दृष्टि</span> (shubh drishti) ho. If Rahu is in 3, 2, 6 then such people are good <span className="hindi">भक्त</span> (bhakt). 9th main rahu matlab <span className="hindi">पिछले जन्म में गुरु द्रोह</span> (pichle janm main guru droh) or <span className="hindi">उच्च स्वराशि</span> (Uccha swarashi). <span className="hindi">दूसरे की practice से न हिले वो व्यक्ति</span> (Doosre ki practice se na hile wo vyakti). Brings a lot of bad karma. Rahu in 9th: <span className="hindi">अभाग्यशाली</span> (abhagyashaali). |
| <span className="hindi">केतु</span> (Ketu) | 4th house | Ketu = detachment. Fire ka ketu in water. Ketu brings <span className="hindi">मोक्ष</span> (moksha) in materialistic things. People are not able to go heart to heart with someone. Whenever they crave for something, ketu deprive you of it. Rahu in 10th. Indulged in work, don't enjoy anything. Good for spirituality. Ketu 4. Rahu 10 is good for spiritual growth. |
