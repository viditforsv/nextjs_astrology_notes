## Characteristics

Chitra represents brilliance, beauty, and the power to create beautiful forms. It's associated with architecture, design, and aesthetic perfection.

### Positive Traits
- Highly creative and artistic
- Eye for beauty and design
- Charismatic and attractive
- Intelligent and perceptive
- Ambitious and driven
- Natural sense of style
- Good at creating things
- Magnetic personality

### Negative Traits
- Can be superficial
- Overly concerned with appearances
- May be vain or egotistical
- Impatient with imperfection
- Can be manipulative
- May lack depth

## Ruling Deity: Tvashtar

Tvashtar (also Vishwakarma) represents:
- Divine architecture and design
- Creative manifestation
- Form and beauty
- Cosmic craftsmanship

## Career Paths

- Engineering and construction

Chitra is excellent for:

- Purchasing jewelry or gems
- Starting construction
- Creative endeavors

The bright jewel symbolizes the hidden beauty that can be revealed through careful craftsmanship. People born under Chitra have an innate understanding of form, color, and aesthetic principles. They can see beauty where others cannot and have the skill to manifest their visions into reality.

This nakshatra bridges the practical Virgo and aesthetic Libra, combining technical precision with artistic beauty.
