# 11th Bhava

## Basic Information

- **Sanskrit Name**: <span className="hindi">लाभ भाव</span> (Gains House)
- **Significator**: <span className="hindi">बुध</span> (Mercury)
- **Classification**: <span className="hindi">उपचय</span> (Upachaya/Growth House)
- **Note**: Wealth - 2nd house. Income(11th) - Expenditure = Wealth(2nd house)<br/>Free will here also<br/>Earn a lot and also punish a lot
