# Avatars (Dashavatara)

The ten incarnations of <span className="hindi">विष्णु</span> (Vishnu) and their planetary associations in Vedic Astrology.

## The Ten Avatars

1. **Matsya** (The Fish) - Associated with <span className="hindi">केतु</span> (Ketu)
2. **Kurma** (The Tortoise) - Associated with <span className="hindi">राहु</span> (Rahu)
3. **Varaha** (The Boar) - Associated with <span className="hindi">शनि</span> (Saturn)
4. **Narasimha** (The Man-Lion) - Associated with <span className="hindi">मंगल</span> (Mars)
5. **Vamana** (The Dwarf) - Associated with <span className="hindi">गुरु</span> (Jupiter)
6. **Parashurama** (Rama with the Axe) - Associated with <span className="hindi">शुक्र</span> (Venus)
7. **Rama** (The Prince of Ayodhya) - Associated with <span className="hindi">सूर्य</span> (Sun)
8. **Krishna** (The Divine Cowherd) - Associated with <span className="hindi">चंद्र</span> (Moon)
9. **Buddha** (The Enlightened One) - Associated with <span className="hindi">बुध</span> (Mercury)
10. **Kalki** (The Future Warrior) - Yet to appear
