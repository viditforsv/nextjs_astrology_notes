# ✋ Hasta Nakshatra

## Overview

- **Number**: 13th Nakshatra
- **Symbol**: Hand, Fist, Palm
- **Deity**: Savitar (Sun God, Divine Craftsman)
- **Ruling Planet**: Moon
- **Zodiac Sign**: Virgo (10°00' - 23°20')
- **Element**: Earth
- **Guna**: Sattva (Purity)
- **Nature**: Light (Laghu), Swift
- **Caste**: Vaishya (Merchant)
- **Gender**: Male
- **Body Part**: Hands, fingers, arms

## Characteristics

Hasta represents skill, dexterity, and the power of the hand. It's associated with craftsmanship, healing, and the ability to create and manifest.

### Positive Traits
- Skilled with hands
- Clever and intelligent
- Hardworking and industrious
- Good sense of humor
- Adaptable and resourceful
- Honest in dealings
- Quick learners
- Healing touch

### Negative Traits
- Can be too clever or cunning
- May use skills manipulatively
- Restless and anxious
- Overly critical
- Difficulty trusting others
- May be mischievous

## Ruling Deity: Savitar

Savitar represents:
- Divine craftsmanship
- Skill and dexterity
- Creative power
- Transformation through skill

## Career Paths

People with strong Hasta influence excel in:
- Handcrafts and artisan work
- Surgery and healthcare
- Massage therapy and healing
- Pottery, sculpting, painting
- Magic and sleight of hand
- Trading and commerce
- Writing and calligraphy
- Technology and gadgets
- Astrology and palmistry

## Compatibility

### Most Compatible
- Ashwini, Rohini, Pushya, Uttara Phalguni, Chitra, Swati, Anuradha, Shravana, Revati

### Least Compatible
- Ardra, Mula, Shatabhisha

## Special Notes

Hasta is excellent for:
- Learning new skills
- Starting craft businesses
- Buying tools and equipment
- Healing practices
- Trading activities
- Signing documents

The symbol of the hand represents the power to create, heal, bless, and manifest. People born under Hasta have a natural gift for working with their hands and often excel in fields requiring manual dexterity and skill. This nakshatra is particularly favorable for all activities involving hands and fingers.
