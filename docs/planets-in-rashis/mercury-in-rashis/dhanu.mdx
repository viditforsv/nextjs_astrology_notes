## Key Factors

### Digbala (Directional Strength)
- <span className="hindi">बुध</span> has **Digbala in 1st House** (North direction)
- Mercury gains maximum directional strength
- Enhances philosophical communication and teaching ability
- Good for spreading knowledge and dharma

### Dwiswabhavi Nature
- <span className="hindi">बुध</span> is **Dwiswabhavi** (dual nature planet)
- Adaptable and versatile
- Balances logic with philosophy
- Intellectual flexibility

### Enemy Relationship
- <span className="hindi">बुध</span> and <span className="hindi">गुरु</span> are **enemies**
- Mercury (logic, details, facts) vs Jupiter (wisdom, expansion, faith)
- Creates internal conflict:
  - Logic vs faith
  - Details vs big picture
  - Analysis vs synthesis
  - Skepticism vs belief
- Despite enmity, can create **logical philosophy**
- Digbala helps overcome this conflict

### Movable Sign Nature
- <span className="hindi">धनु</span> is **Chara (Movable) Rashi**
- Movable fire = adventurous, exploring, expanding
- Mercury's intellect seeks new horizons
- Creates: Philosophical thinking, teaching orientation, travel-focused communication

### Lordship Impact (7th and 10th Lord in 1st)

**7th Lord in 1st House:**
- Partnerships define personality
- Spouse very important to identity
- Business-oriented nature
- Public dealings central
- Marriage shapes life direction
- Partner influences you strongly
- Good for business partnerships
- Dependent on others for success
- Attractive to opposite sex

**10th Lord in 1st House:**
- **Raja Yoga** - powerful combination
- Career defines personality
- Professional success strong
- High status and reputation
- Authority and leadership
- Public recognition
- Self-made career achievements
- Work is identity

**Combined Effect (7L + 10L in 1H):**
- **Extremely powerful combination**
- Career through partnerships (business)
- Spouse helps career
- Public image through relationships
- Marriage boosts professional status
- **Excellent for business and public life**
- Partnership-based success
- Fame through marriage/business
