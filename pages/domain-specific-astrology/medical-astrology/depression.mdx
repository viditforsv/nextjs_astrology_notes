# Depression

## Causes

- <span className="hindi">चंद्र</span> (Moon) and Me (native) under the influence of malefic <span className="hindi">ग्रह</span> (graha) or misplaced
- 5H (5th House) and 5L (5th Lord) under the influence of malefic

## Can Come Out

- If <span className="hindi">बुध</span> (Mercury) is not under the influence of malefic
- Cyclical swings into depression
- Uses <span className="hindi">बुध</span> (Mercury) and <span className="hindi">बुद्धि</span> (Buddhi) to come out

## Cannot Come Out

- If <span className="hindi">बुध</span> (Mercury) is under the influence of malefic
