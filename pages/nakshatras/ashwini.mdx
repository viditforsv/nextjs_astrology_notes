## Characteristics

Ashwini is the first nakshatra, symbolizing new beginnings, healing, and swift action. People born under this nakshatra are known for their:

### Positive Traits
- Quick and energetic
- Pioneering spirit
- Natural healers
- Adventurous nature
- Independent and self-reliant
- Good at initiating projects
- Attractive and charming personality

### Negative Traits
- Impatient and restless
- May lack follow-through
- Can be impulsive
- Stubborn at times
- May struggle with consistency

## Ruling Deity: Ashwini Kumaras

The Ashwini Kumaras are twin brothers who are celestial physicians. They represent:
- Healing and medicine
- Speed and agility
- Youthfulness and vitality
- Divine intervention

## Career Paths

People with strong Ashwini influence excel in:
- Medicine and healthcare
- Sports and athletics
- Military and defense
- Transportation industry
- Veterinary sciences
- Emergency services
- Racing and competitive fields
