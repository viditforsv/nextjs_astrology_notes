# Stock Market Analysis

## Timing Principles

### Short-Term (Intraday/Hourly)
- **<span className="hindi">चंद्र</span> (Moon):** Market sentiment, volatility
- **<span className="hindi">बुध</span> (Mercury):** Information flow, trading activity

### Long-Term Investing

**<span className="hindi">गुरु</span> (Jupiter) - Primary Indicator**

⚠️ **Avoid When:**
1. Jupiter in Kendra from malefics (<span className="hindi">मंगल</span>, <span className="hindi">शनि</span>, <span className="hindi">राहु</span>, <span className="hindi">केतु</span>)
2. Jupiter combust, debilitated, or heavily afflicted

✅ **Ideal:**
- Exalted (<span className="hindi">कर्क</span>)
- Own sign (<span className="hindi">धनु</span>, <span className="hindi">मीन</span>)
- Strong in Kendra from Lagna
- Protected, no malefic Kendra relationship
