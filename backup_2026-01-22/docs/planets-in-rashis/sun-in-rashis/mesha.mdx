## Remedies & Integration

### Balance the Fire
**The exalted Sun's brilliance must be channeled properly:**

1. **Shaucha (Purity)** - Discipline energy through clean habits and routines
2. **Svadhyaya (Self-study)** - Humility through scriptures keeps ego in check
3. **Ishvara Pranidhana (Surrender)** - Offer authority as service, not possession
4. **Santosha (Contentment)** - Balance emotional fire with inner peace
5. **Tapas (Discipline)** - Use authority with discipline, not attachment

### Practical Remedies
- **Surya Namaskar** (Sun salutation) daily
- **Gayatri Mantra** at sunrise
- Offer water to Sun in morning
- Ruby gemstone (if suitable)
- Fast on Sundays
- Respect father and authority figures
- Practice humility despite natural authority
- Channel leadership for dharma, not ego
