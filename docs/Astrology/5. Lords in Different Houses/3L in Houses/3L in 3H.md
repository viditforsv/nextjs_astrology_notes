* If the **3rd lord is in the 3rd house**, results depend on strength:

  * If in **own sign (swarashi)** → the house becomes powerful.
  * Example: In Libra ascendant, Jupiter as 3rd lord in 3rd house.

* **General results:**

  * Happiness from siblings.
  * Wealthy through **self-effort** (since 3rd → effort, 2nd → wealth, so wealth comes from effort).
  * Cheerful and happy.
  * Gain of children (3rd is 11th from 5th = gain of child). If badly placed, difficulties in children.

* **3rd house also = hobbies, adventure, excitement, travel.**

  * Good placement for becoming a **player, musician, writer, singer**.
  * Specific planet sitting there decides the kind of talent:

    * **Jupiter in 3rd** → rosary in hand → religious, mantra-related.
    * **Mars in 3rd** → energy, courage, sports, police, printing (ink).
    * **Moon as 3rd lord in 3rd** → great singing talent (example: Lata Mangeshkar).
    * **Venus** → beautician, painter, artistic/visual talents. Venus = images, pictures.
    * **Mercury in 3rd** → pen in hand → writing. With Ketu, can write software, thrillers, horror.
    * **Saturn in 3rd** → tragedy, sorrowful expression in writing. Creates destiny by aspecting 9th.

* **Performing arts:**

  * Painting, dancing, singing → strongly linked with this placement.
  * Venus + Mars in 3rd → acting, event management, performing arts.

* **Event management explanation:**

  * Mars = physical effort (lifting, standing, organizing).
  * Saturn = perfection, discipline (a delay or mistake ruins event).
  * Venus = art, beauty, creativity.
  * Together, Venus + Mars + Saturn in 3rd are excellent for event management.
