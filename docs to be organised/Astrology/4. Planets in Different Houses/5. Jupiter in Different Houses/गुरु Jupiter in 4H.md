## Jupiter in 4th House

- **House meaning**: Happiness, mother, home, mind, direction (gati).
- **Special note**:

  - Cancer in Kalpurush = exaltation for Jupiter → considered one of best placements.

- **Gives**:

  - Peace of mind (true happiness).
  - Balanced pleasure — does not overindulge.
  - Home feels like an ashram.
  - Good material comforts (property, vehicles, good spouse).

- **Aspects from 4th House**:

  - 10th: Makes native a good consultant/teacher; career guided by ethics; no wrong deeds for money.
  - 8th: Removes obstacles from sudden events (death, loss, defamation).
  - 12th: Spiritual orientation, detachment from material overindulgence.

- **Moksha Trikon link**: Controls desires; shifts focus to spiritual life.
- **Mother’s role**:

  - Planet here shows mother’s nature; Jupiter’s motherly form = Goddess Tara — praying to Tara or Jupiter gives life direction.

- **Staan Hani principle here**:

  - Reduces mind’s mood-orientation, increases wisdom-orientation.
  - Person does what’s necessary, not just what’s desired.

- **Possible drawback**:

  - Always doing the right thing may cause stress or be challenging for family members.

- **Overall**:

  - One of the finest placements for Jupiter; gives wisdom-led life, property gains, consultancy success, spiritual direction.
