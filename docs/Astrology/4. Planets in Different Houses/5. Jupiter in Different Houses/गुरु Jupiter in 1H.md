## Jupiter in 1st House (Lagna)

- **Nature & Role**: Karaka of _Dhi Shakti_ (knowledge), giver of results in many areas.
- **Impact**: If strong in Lagna, removes “sawa lakh dosh” (1,25,000 defects) in life.
- **Even with bad planets elsewhere**, Jupiter alone can take a native forward.
- **Significance**: Saturn’s glance = poison, Jupiter’s glance = nectar; brings growth wherever it looks.
- **As Shiva**: Symbol of supreme guru; life guided through knowledge.
- **Longevity**: In Kendra (esp. Lagna), increases lifespan regardless of other factors.
- **Dhi Shakti types**: Buddhi (intellect), Shuddhi (purity), Vriddhi (growth).
- **Aspects from Lagna**:

  - 5th house: Improves past life merit, gives good children and intelligence.
  - 7th house: Improves marriage, meditation, public image; controls Maraka tendencies.
  - 9th house: Enhances fortune, spirituality.

- **Overall**: One of the most beneficial placements; removes obstacles through wisdom.
