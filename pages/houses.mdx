# Houses in Vedic Astrology (Bhavas)

The birth chart is divided into twelve houses (Bhavas), each representing different areas of life.

## The Twelve Houses

### 1st House (Lagna/Ascendant)
- **Significator**: Sun
- **Represents**: Self, personality, physical body, overall life path
- **Nature**: Tanu Bhava (Body House)
- **Classification**: Kendra (Angular), Trikona (Trinal)

### 2nd House
- **Significator**: Jupiter
- **Represents**: Wealth, family, speech, food, early education
- **Nature**: Dhana Bhava (Wealth House)
- **Classification**: Marak (Death-inflicting)

### 3rd House
- **Significator**: Mars
- **Represents**: Siblings, courage, communication, short travels, skills
- **Nature**: Sahaj Bhava (Siblings House)
- **Classification**: Upachaya (Growth House)

### 4th House
- **Significator**: Moon
- **Represents**: Mother, home, property, vehicles, emotions, education
- **Nature**: Sukha Bhava (Happiness House)
- **Classification**: Kendra (Angular)

### 5th House
- **Significator**: Jupiter
- **Represents**: Children, creativity, intelligence, romance, past life karma
- **Nature**: Putra Bhava (Children House)
- **Classification**: Trikona (Trinal)

### 6th House
- **Significator**: Mars, Saturn
- **Represents**: Enemies, diseases, debts, service, daily work
- **Nature**: Ripu Bhava (Enemy House)
- **Classification**: Dusthana (Difficult House), Upachaya

### 7th House
- **Significator**: Venus
- **Represents**: Spouse, marriage, partnerships, business
- **Nature**: Kalatra Bhava (Spouse House)
- **Classification**: Kendra (Angular), Marak

### 8th House
- **Significator**: Saturn
- **Represents**: Longevity, transformation, occult, inheritance, sudden events
- **Nature**: Ayur Bhava (Longevity House)
- **Classification**: Dusthana (Difficult House)

### 9th House
- **Significator**: Jupiter, Sun
- **Represents**: Father, guru, fortune, dharma, long travels, higher education
- **Nature**: Bhagya Bhava (Fortune House)
- **Classification**: Trikona (Trinal)

### 10th House
- **Significator**: Sun, Mercury, Jupiter, Saturn
- **Represents**: Career, status, reputation, karma
- **Nature**: Karma Bhava (Career House)
- **Classification**: Kendra (Angular)

### 11th House
- **Significator**: Jupiter
- **Represents**: Gains, income, friends, aspirations, elder siblings
- **Nature**: Labha Bhava (Gains House)
- **Classification**: Upachaya (Growth House)

### 12th House
- **Significator**: Saturn
- **Represents**: Losses, expenses, foreign lands, spirituality, moksha, isolation
- **Nature**: Vyaya Bhava (Loss House)
- **Classification**: Dusthana (Difficult House)

## House Classifications

### Kendras (Angular Houses)
- 1st, 4th, 7th, 10th
- Most powerful houses
- Provide strength and stability

### Trikonas (Trinal Houses)
- 1st, 5th, 9th
- Auspicious houses
- Dharma, fortune, and self

### Dusthanas (Difficult Houses)
- 6th, 8th, 12th
- Challenging areas
- Growth through difficulty

### Upachaya (Growth Houses)
- 3rd, 6th, 10th, 11th
- Improve with time
- Malefics do well here

### Maraka (Death-inflicting Houses)
- 2nd, 7th
- Can cause health issues during dashas
