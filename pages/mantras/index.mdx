# Mantras

Mantras are sacred sounds, words, or phrases used in meditation and spiritual practice.

## General Principles

- **Any mantra purifies the mind** (<span className="hindi">कोई भी मंत्र मन को तर कर देता है</span>)
- **Any mantra increases patience, perseverance, and compassion**
- **Hundreds of things get impacted by mantras**
- **Practice with <span className="hindi">श्रद्धा</span> (Shraddha - faith/devotion)**

## Practice Guidelines

- **Mantras should be done with light or empty stomach**

## Listening vs Chanting

- **Listening is equally beneficial as chanting**, provided that your attention is there

## Key Mantras

- **[Gayatri Mantra](/mantras/gayatri-mantra)** - Removes all kinds of pain
- **[Mahamrityunjaya Mantra](/mantras/mahamrityunjaya-mantra)** - Can be used to revive anything that is dead, even non-living entities like businesses. Has tremendous benefits for health.
