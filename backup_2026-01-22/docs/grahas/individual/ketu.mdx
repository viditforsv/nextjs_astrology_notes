# Ketu (केतु) ☋

## Basic Information

- **Name**: Ketu (South Node of Moon)
- **Nature**: Malefic (but spiritually benefic), Shadow Planet
- **Element**: Fire (Agni)
- **Direction**: Northwest
- **Metal**: Cat's Eye stone
- **Color**: Brown, Grey, Multicolored
- **Gemstone**: Cat's Eye (Lehsunia)
- **Day**: Tuesday (shares with Mars)
- **Gender**: Male
- **Varna**: Outcaste (Mleccha)
- **Motion**: Always retrograde (moves backward)

## Planetary Details

- **Exaltation**: <span className="hindi">वृश्चिक</span> (occult, sadhu, spirituality)
- **Debilitation**: <span className="hindi">मेष</span>
- **Moolatrikona**: <span className="hindi">मीन</span> (some traditions)
- **Own Sign**: None (shadow planet)
- **Friends**: <span className="hindi">मंगल</span>, <span className="hindi">शुक्र</span>, <span className="hindi">शनि</span>
- **Enemies**: <span className="hindi">सूर्य</span>, <span className="hindi">चंद्र</span>
- **Neutral**: <span className="hindi">बुध</span>, <span className="hindi">गुरु</span>

## Dasha Period
7 years in Vimshottari Dasha

## Drishti (Aspect)
- **No aspect** (Ketu has no head = no vision)
- Acts through presence and conjunction rather than aspect

## Core Nature

- **South Node** of Moon's orbit
- **Shadow planet** (no physical body)
- Headless body - acts on instinct
- Represents:
  - **Moksha** (Spiritual liberation)
  - **Past life karma**
  - **Detachment**

## Significations (Karaka)

### Spiritual
- **Moksha** (Liberation)
- Spirituality and renunciation
- Occult knowledge
- Mysticism and tantra
- Past life experiences
- Enlightenment

### Modern
- **Software** and technology
- **Buildings** and construction
- Research and investigation
- Unconventional knowledge

### General
- Detachment
- Sudden events
- Mystical experiences
- Hidden knowledge
- Spiritual practices

## Special Characteristics

### Ketu + Sun
- **Ketu loves Surya** (both Sattvic)
- **Ketu eclipses the Sun** (not gets combust)
- **Ketu doesn't get combust** - it combusts/eclipses Sun instead
- **Sun hates Rahu** but comfortable with Ketu
- Ketu + Surya especially good for spirituality in 8th house

### Ketu + Moon
- Chandra + Ketu gives deep spiritual insight
- Emotional detachment leading to wisdom
- Intuitive spiritual understanding

## Ketu in 8th House

Special placement:
- **Mystical death** and transformation
- Hidden knowledge
- Spiritual power
- Connection to:
  - Astrology
  - Mysticism
  - Occult sciences
- Need to be careful of **accidents**
- Strong spiritual progress

## Exaltation & Debilitation

### Exalted in Scorpio (Occult & Spirituality)
- Moksha orientation
- Sadhu-like qualities
- Deep mystical knowledge
- Transformation and occult mastery

### Debilitated in Aries
- Spiritual energy meets aggressive action
- Conflict between detachment and assertion
- Difficult for Ketu's nature

## Ketu vs Rahu

| Aspect | Rahu | Ketu |
|--------|------|------|
| **Direction** | Future, desire | Past, detachment |
| **Nature** | Worldly obsession | Spiritual liberation |
| **Head/Body** | Head (thinks/desires) | Body (acts on instinct) |
| **Goal** | Material fulfillment | Moksha |
| **Knowledge** | Superficial, scattered | Deep, focused |
| **Energy** | Expanding confusion | Collapsing into truth |

## Mythology

- **Tail of demon** Swarbhanu
- Body without head after beheading by Vishnu
- Acts on spiritual instinct
- Represents the karmic past
- No eyes to see - works through inner knowing

## General Effects

- Creates sudden events and surprises
- Brings spiritual breakthroughs
- Cuts material attachments
- Gives research ability
- Psychic and intuitive abilities
- Interest in mysticism and occult
- May cause confusion or lack of direction
- Ultimate goal is liberation

## Remedies

### Mantras

### Strengthening Ketu (When Favorable)
- Worship Lord Ganesha (remover of obstacles)
- Worship Lord Shiva
- Practice meditation and yoga
- Donate to spiritual causes
- Wear Cat's Eye (after consultation)
- Feed and help stray dogs
- Light ghee lamp on Tuesdays
- Chant Ganesha mantras

### Managing Ketu's Energy
- Embrace spirituality
- Practice detachment consciously
- Study occult sciences properly
- Ground yourself in practical matters
- Balance material and spiritual
- Seek guidance from realized masters
