1. **Strength of Placement**

   * 3rd house = *Parakram* (courage, effort).
   * Malefics do well in 3, 6, 11 → Mars in 3rd is considered **very strong**.
   * Person becomes highly courageous and valorous.

2. **Aspects & Enemies**

   * Mars’ aspect falls on 6th house (enemies, debts, diseases).
   * Destroys enemies, gives capacity to fight and win struggles.
   * Suitable for careers like police, army, security, sports, firefighting.

3. **Nature of Efforts**

   * Life becomes full of **struggles requiring effort and fight**.
   * Victory comes through hard work and courage, but stress remains.
   * Liberation for such a person is through *parakram* (continuous effort).

4. **Career Link**

   * Aspect on 10th house gives power to execute and achieve in profession.
   * Makes person hardworking, always driven to perform and win.

5. **Impact on Siblings**

   * Not good for younger siblings.
   * Either siblings struggle in life, or relations with them get strained.
   * If 3rd lord is weak, sibling relations suffer more.

6. **Adventure & Sports**

   * Gives love for sports, adventure, physical activities.
   * Person enjoys challenges, travel, trekking, mountaineering, adventurous tasks.

7. **Rebellious Thinking**

   * Mars in 3rd aspects 9th house (religion, philosophy).
   * Makes person question religion, authority, and traditions.
   * Less inclined to rituals, temple visits, or meditation unless convinced intellectually.
   * Mars being technical → always asks “why?” until satisfied.
