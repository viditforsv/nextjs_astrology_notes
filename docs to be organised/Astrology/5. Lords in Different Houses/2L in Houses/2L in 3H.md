### 3rd House
- You can work in domains of 3H
- Earn through Art, drama, music
- Take help from brothers
- Money going to chota bhai
- Malefic
	- Lose money to chota bhai
- Benefic
	- Gain money from chota bhai
- 2nd of 2H, Dhan kamana hai to risk lena padega. 
	- Courageous in terms of money
	- If malefic then kanjoos. 
- Native can be lustful if malefic
- Drishti on 10H
	- person can communicate or have speech towards religious activities
- 2L in 3H looking at 9H

1. **Nature of 3rd House**

   * 3rd house = creativity, talents, communication, adventure, travel, courage.
   * When 2nd lord (wealth) goes to 3rd house → money connects with creativity, arts, travel, communication, etc.

2. **Sources of Earning (Planet-based)**

   * Depends on which planet is the 2nd lord:

     * With Mercury + Ketu → software.
     * With Jupiter → philosophy.
     * With Sun → technical, government.
     * With Venus → travel.
     * With Moon → arts.
   * General: earning through art, drama, music, creativity, or with help of younger brothers.

3. **If Afflicted**

   * Money goes to younger brother but gets wasted if afflicted.
   * Conflict and loss of wealth through siblings.

4. **If Benefic**

   * Gains money through abilities and support of younger brothers.

5. **General Traits**

   * Person becomes courageous, knowledgeable, virtuous, lustful, and miserly.

6. **Bhavat Bhavam Principle**

   * 2nd of 2nd = 3rd house.
   * Meaning: to gain money, one must take risks (3rd = house of adventure, effort, parakram).
   * In business, risk = weapon.
   * If benefic → makes person even stronger in savings (principle + interest).
   * Hence, becomes a miser (focus on saving).
   * If afflicted → spends everything, loses wealth.

7. **Lust Aspect**

   * 2nd house is 8th from 7th (linked with sex).
   * 3rd house itself is also a house of sex (Maithun bhava).
   * If afflicted 2nd lord in 3rd → person becomes very lustful.

8. **Aspect on 9th House**

   * 2nd lord in 3rd always aspects the 9th.
   * If benefic → earnings through teaching, training, spirituality, religious work, education, social work.
   * Uses 2nd (speech), 3rd (communication), for 10th (karma) and 11th (income).
   * Placement supports careers in education, spirituality, communication-driven professions.
