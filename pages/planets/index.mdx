# Planets in Vedic Astrology (Navagrahas)

In Vedic Astrology, nine planets (Navagrahas) are considered, including two shadow planets (Rahu and Ketu).

## The Nine Planets

| Planet | Sanskrit | Nature | Represents |
|--------|----------|--------|------------|
| Sun | Surya | Malefic (when afflicted) | Soul, Father, Authority |
| Moon | Chandra | Benefic | Mind, Mother, Emotions |
| Mars | Mangala | Malefic | Energy, Courage, Siblings |
| Mercury | Budha | Neutral | Intelligence, Communication |
| Jupiter | Guru | Benefic | Wisdom, Spirituality, Teacher |
| Venus | Shukra | Benefic | Love, Beauty, Luxury |
| Saturn | Shani | Malefic | Discipline, Karma, Delays |
| Rahu | Rahu | Malefic | Obsession, Illusion, Foreign |
| Ketu | Ketu | Malefic | Spirituality, Moksha, Past Life |

## Classification

### Natural Benefics
- Jupiter (greatest benefic)
- Venus
- Mercury (when well-placed)
- Waxing Moon

### Natural Malefics
- Saturn (greatest malefic)
- Mars
- Rahu
- Ketu
- Sun
- Waning Moon

### Temporal Benefics/Malefics
The nature of planets also changes based on house rulership for each ascendant.

## Important Planetary States

- **Exaltation (Uchcha)**: Planet at maximum strength
- **Debilitation (Neecha)**: Planet at minimum strength
- **Moolatrikona**: Own sign with special dignity
- **Own Sign (Swakshetra)**: Comfortable placement
- **Friend's Sign**: Positive placement
- **Enemy's Sign**: Challenging placement
