### 3rd Aspect (Trtiya Drishti) - House of Struggle
- You must exert effort

- Take enterprise-style action
- Examples:
  - Sa in 6H, 3A on 8H: Will destroy kashta

### 7th Aspect (Saptam Drishti) - Full Bullet
- Sorrow or conflict in partnerships
- Hard battles with "others"
- Maximum pressure, maximum mehnat
- Delay and disappointment
- Extreme discipline needed, then reward
- Examples:
  - Sa in 7H, 7A on 1H: Can lead to depression
  - Sa in 10H, 7A on 4H: No happiness at home, suffering from mother
  - Sa in 6H, 7A on 12H: Kanjoos (miserly), avoids foreign lands

### 10th Aspect (Dasham Drishti) - Pending Karma
- Karma must be paid life-long
- Shrinkage, delays, lack of recognition
- Karma remains pending in that house
- Examples:
  - Sa in 6H, 10A on 3H: Struggles end (good placement)
  - Sa in 10H, 10A on 7H: Pending karma toward spouse, no recognition
  - Marriage won't break because karma must be completed
