# Rahu (राहु) ☊

## Basic Information

- **Name**: Rahu (North Node of Moon)
- **Nature**: Malefic, Shadow Planet
- **Element**: Air (Vayu)
- **Direction**: Southwest
- **Metal**: Lead
- **Color**: Dark Blue, Black, Smoky
- **Gemstone**: Hessonite (Gomed/Gomedh)
- **Day**: Saturday (shares with Saturn)
- **Gender**: Male
- **Varna**: Outcaste (Mleccha)
- **Motion**: Always retrograde (moves backward)

## Planetary Details

- **Exaltation**: 
  - <span className="hindi">वृषभ</span> (Principle 1) - Accumulation of wealth
  - <span className="hindi">मिथुन</span> (Principle 2) - Adventure, initiation, parakram
- **Debilitation**: 
  - <span className="hindi">वृश्चिक</span> (Principle 1)
  - <span className="hindi">धनु</span> (Principle 2) - In <span className="hindi">गुरु</span>'s home (prime enemies)
- **Moolatrikona**: <span className="hindi">कन्या</span> (some traditions)
- **Own Sign**: None (shadow planet)
- **Friends**: <span className="hindi">शुक्र</span>, <span className="hindi">शनि</span>
- **Enemies**: <span className="hindi">सूर्य</span>, <span className="hindi">चंद्र</span>, <span className="hindi">मंगल</span>

## Dasha Period
18 years in Vimshottari Dasha

## Drishti (Special Aspects)
- **5th House** - Like Jupiter (but with Maya)
- **7th House** - Full aspect
- **9th House** - Like Jupiter (but creates dur-bhagya)

Rahu aspects like Jupiter, but the nature is opposite:
- Creates **hallucination** and **Maya (illusion)**
- **Iccha puri hoti lagegi nahi** (Desire seems fulfilled but isn't)
- Cannot enjoy the fulfillment even if achieved

## Core Nature

- **South Node** of Moon's orbit
- **Shadow planet** (no physical body)
- Represents:
  - **Maya** (Illusion)
  - **Brahm** (Confusion about reality)
  - Unfulfilled desires

## Significations (Karaka)

### Modern & Foreign
- **Foreign lands** and foreign connections
- Scandals and controversies
- Software and technology
- Buildings and construction
- Unconventional paths

### Spiritual
- **Moksha** (paradoxically, through material exhaustion)
- Occult and hidden knowledge
- Breaking conventions
- Liberation through experience

## Rahu's Aspects Explained

### 5th Aspect
- **Purva paap** (past life sins) create problems
- Native won't be able to leave because it's desire-based
- Example: Rahu 5A on 2H
  - Hallucinated for money
  - Desire to make money but purva paap blocks it
  - May eat things that shouldn't be eaten
  - Interest in smoking and alcohol

### 7th Aspect
- Creates illusions in partnerships
- Desire-driven relationships
- May not be what they seem

### 9th Aspect (Dur-Bhagya)
- **Misfortune** in that house
- **Galat guru** (Wrong teacher) - misleading guidance
- You will go there because of desire
- **Be very careful** selecting teachers in those domains
- Don't finalize yourself, take help from trusted advisors

## Rahu Drishti Creates Karma

- Ra ki drishti is creating karma for the next birth
- Can be transformed with awareness
- Wherever Rahu looks, be mindful of desires
- Desire without wisdom leads to future bondage

## Exaltation & Debilitation

### Exalted in Taurus (In Venus' Home)
- Accumulation of wealth
- Material desires fulfilled
- Love for luxury and accumulation

### Exalted in Gemini
- Adventure and initiation
- Parakram (courage and enterprise)
- Communication of unconventional ideas

### Debilitated in Scorpio & Sagittarius
- In Guru's home (prime enemies)
- Rahu and Guru are natural enemies
- Philosophical/spiritual conflict with material desires

## General Characteristics

- Always moving backward (retrograde motion)
- Creates obsession and compulsion
- Amplifies wherever it sits
- Foreign connections and foreign travels
- Technology and modern innovations
- Breaks traditions and conventions

## Mythology

- **Head of demon** Swarbhanu
- Beheaded by Lord Vishnu during Samudra Manthan
- Head became Rahu (north node)
- Eternally chasing Sun and Moon (causing eclipses)

## Remedies

### Mantras
```
Om Rahave Namah
Om Bhram (beej mantra)
```

### Transformation of Rahu
- Worship Goddess Durga
- Chant Durga Saptashati
- Donate to outcasts and underprivileged
- Serve lepers or those with skin diseases
- Feed dogs and street animals
- Saturday fasting
- Wear Hessonite after proper consultation

### Managing Rahu's Illusions
- Practice discernment
- Don't rush into desires
- Seek proper guidance
- Transform maya into awareness
- Spiritual practices to ground energy
