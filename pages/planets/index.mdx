# Planets in Vedic Astrology (Navagrahas)

In Vedic Astrology, nine planets (Navagrahas) are considered, including two shadow planets (Rahu and Ketu).

## The Nine Planets

| Planet | Hindi Name | Nature | Represents |
|--------|----------|--------|------------|
| [Sun](/planets/sun) | <span className="hindi">सूर्य</span> | Malefic (when afflicted) | Soul, Father, Authority |
| [Moon](/planets/moon) | <span className="hindi">चंद्र</span> | Benefic | Mind, Mother, Emotions |
| [Mars](/planets/mars) | <span className="hindi">मंगल</span> | Malefic | Energy, Courage, Siblings |
| [Mercury](/planets/mercury) | <span className="hindi">बुध</span> | Neutral | Intelligence, Communication |
| [Jupiter](/planets/jupiter) | <span className="hindi">गुरु</span> | Benefic | Wisdom, Spirituality, Teacher |
| [Venus](/planets/venus) | <span className="hindi">शुक्र</span> | Benefic | Love, Beauty, Luxury |
| [Saturn](/planets/saturn) | <span className="hindi">शनि</span> | Malefic | Discipline, Karma, Delays |
| [Rahu](/planets/rahu) | <span className="hindi">राहु</span> | Malefic | Obsession, Illusion, Foreign |
| [Ketu](/planets/ketu) | <span className="hindi">केतु</span> | Malefic | Spirituality, Moksha, Past Life |

## Classification

### Natural Benefics
- Jupiter (greatest benefic)
- Venus
- Mercury (when well-placed)
- Waxing Moon

### Natural Malefics
- Saturn (greatest malefic)
- Mars
- Rahu
- Ketu
- Sun
- Waning Moon

### Temporal Benefics/Malefics
The nature of planets also changes based on house rulership for each ascendant.

## Important Planetary States

- **Exaltation (Uchcha)**: Planet at maximum strength
- **Moolatrikona**: Own sign with special dignity
- **Own Sign (Swarashi)**: Comfortable placement
- **Friend's Sign**: Positive placement
- **Enemy's Sign**: Challenging placement
- **Debilitation (Neecha)**: Planet at minimum strength
