## Key Factors

### Digbala (Directional Strength)
- <span className="hindi">बुध</span> has **Digbala in 1st House** (North direction)
- Mercury gains maximum directional strength in this placement
- Enhances intelligence, communication, and business acumen
- Very favorable for intellectual pursuits and self-expression

### Dwiswabhavi Nature
- <span className="hindi">बुध</span> is **Dwiswabhavi** (dual nature planet)
- Adaptable and flexible
- Can take on qualities of companions
- Mutable and changeable

### Enemy Relationship
- <span className="hindi">बुध</span> and <span className="hindi">मंगल</span> are **enemies**
- Mercury (intellect, peace) vs Mars (action, aggression)
- Creates internal conflict:
  - Mind wants to think, body wants to act
  - Analysis paralysis vs impulsive action
  - Peaceful nature in aggressive sign
- Despite enmity, Digbala compensates significantly

### Lordship Impact (3rd and 6th Lord in 1st)

**3rd Lord in 1st House:**
- Self-made person, self-efforts bring results
- Communication becomes part of personality
- Courageous and initiative-taking nature
- Good for younger siblings (beneficial placement)
- Skills and hobbies define the person
- Short travels frequently

**6th Lord in 1st House:**
- Health issues possible (6th = diseases)
- Competitive nature, fights enemies
- Service-oriented personality
- May face obstacles related to self
- Strong immunity if well-placed
- Daily work/service becomes identity
- Debts and loans affect personality

**Combined Effect (3L + 6L in 1H):**
- Self-reliant fighter
- Overcomes obstacles through own efforts (3H courage + 6H enemies in 1H)
- May have health issues but fights through them
- Communication used to deal with enemies/competition
- Service through self-expression
