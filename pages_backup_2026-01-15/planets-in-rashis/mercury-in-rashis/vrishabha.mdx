## Key Factors

### Digbala (Directional Strength)
- <span className="hindi">बुध</span> has **Digbala in 1st House** (North direction)
- Mercury gains maximum directional strength in this placement
- Enhances intelligence, communication, and practical wisdom
- Very favorable for wealth and speech

### Dwiswabhavi Nature
- <span className="hindi">बुध</span> is **Dwiswabhavi** (dual nature planet)
- Adaptable yet grounded
- Balances flexibility with stability
- Practical intelligence

### Friend Relationship
- <span className="hindi">बुध</span> and <span className="hindi">शुक्र</span> are **friends**
- Mercury (intellect) + Venus (beauty/art) = harmonious blend
- Creates:
  - Artistic communication
  - Beautiful speech
  - Practical creativity
  - Business acumen for luxury/beauty
- Intelligence applied to material success
- Grounded, pleasant personality

### Fixed Sign Nature
- <span className="hindi">वृषभ</span> is **Sthira (Fixed) Rashi**
- Fixed earth = stable, persistent, material focus
- Mercury's changeability gets stability
- Creates: Steady thinking, persistent learning, reliable communication

### Lordship Impact (2nd and 5th Lord in 1st)

**2nd Lord in 1st House:**
- **Excellent for wealth** - strong dhana yoga
- Speech defines personality
- Beautiful/melodious voice
- Family values important to self
- Food and eating habits prominent
- Early education shapes character
- Face and eyes attractive
- Self-made wealth through personality
- Good for singers, speakers, teachers

**5th Lord in 1st House:**
- **Highly intelligent** - wisdom in personality
- Creative and artistic nature
- Lucky and fortunate (purva punya)
- Good for children (father figure)
- Past life merits manifest
- Speculative gains possible
- Romance and love important
- Natural teacher/advisor
- Strong intuition

**Combined Effect (2L + 5L in 1H):**
- **Extremely auspicious combination** (Dhana + Pancha Mahapurusha)
- Wealth + intelligence in personality
- Beautiful speech brings fortune
- Creative intelligence creates wealth
- Lucky in self-expression
- Children bring joy and prosperity
- Artistic talents define the person
- **One of the best wealth combinations**
