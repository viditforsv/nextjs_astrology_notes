## Water Element Duality

### Venus vs Moon (Two Types of Water)

**Moon:**
- Controls **oceanic water**
- Source of all rain and rivers
- External water

**Venus:**
- Controls **blood/water within body**
- Usable, consumable, internal water
- Bodily fluids

## Retrograde Venus

### Effects
- Romantic karma pending
- First major relationship is **painful, incomplete, or karmic**
- Second relationship brings clarity
- Creates intense desire for love, luxury, sensual experiences
- Emotional relationships often delayed till **after age 30**
- 1st relationship would be painful
- May not want to marry initially
- Would have skills related to Venus
- Two relationships pattern

### Retrograde + Combust Venus
- Powerful yet restricted
- Native with X-factor of Venus but restricted by Sun
- Within bounds of guardian/authority
- Spouse of native may be sickly
- Restricted in relationships - samaj ke saamne restrict ho jaega
- Reserved, calculative, cultured
- Deep cultural discipline

## Combust Venus

### Effects
- Gets combust often due to orbital proximity
- Affects relationships and artistic abilities
- Issues with wife/partner
- Frustration in relationships
- Beauty suppressed by societal norms
- Venus = expression, beauty gets burned near Sun

### Relationship Impacts
- Spouse's health may suffer
- Feel emotionally or publicly restricted in expressing love
- May appear detached in public
- Avoid PDA (Public Display of Affection)
- Feel limited in romantic expression due to social/familial constraints
