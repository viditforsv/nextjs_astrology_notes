# Overview of Bhavas (Houses)

## Introduction

The birth chart is divided into twelve houses (Bhavas), each representing different areas of life. Understanding the houses is fundamental to Vedic Astrology interpretation.

## The Twelve Houses

Each house governs specific aspects of life:

1. **1st House** - <span className="hindi">लग्न</span> (Lagna) - Self, personality, physical body
2. **2nd House** - <span className="hindi">धन भाव</span> (Dhana) - Wealth, family, speech
3. **3rd House** - <span className="hindi">सहज भाव</span> (Sahaj) - Siblings, courage, communication
4. **4th House** - <span className="hindi">सुख भाव</span> (Sukha) - Mother, home, property
5. **5th House** - <span className="hindi">पुत्र भाव</span> (Putra) - Children, creativity, intelligence
6. **6th House** - <span className="hindi">रिपु भाव</span> (Ripu) - Enemies, diseases, service
7. **7th House** - <span className="hindi">कलत्र भाव</span> (Kalatra) - Spouse, marriage, partnerships
8. **8th House** - <span className="hindi">आयु भाव</span> (Ayur) - Longevity, transformation, occult
9. **9th House** - <span className="hindi">भाग्य भाव</span> (Bhagya) - Father, guru, fortune, dharma
10. **10th House** - <span className="hindi">कर्म भाव</span> (Karma) - Career, status, reputation
11. **11th House** - <span className="hindi">लाभ भाव</span> (Labha) - Gains, income, friends
12. **12th House** - <span className="hindi">व्यय भाव</span> (Vyaya) - Losses, expenses, moksha

## House Classifications

### <span className="hindi">केंद्र</span> (Kendra) - Angular Houses
Houses: 1, 4, 7, 10
- Most powerful houses
- Provide strength and stability

### <span className="hindi">त्रिकोण</span> (Trikona) - Trinal Houses
Houses: 1, 5, 9
- Auspicious houses
- Represent dharma, fortune, and self

### <span className="hindi">दुष्टान</span> (Dusthana) - Difficult Houses
Houses: 6, 8, 12
- Challenging areas
- Growth through difficulty

### <span className="hindi">उपचय</span> (Upachaya) - Growth Houses
Houses: 3, 6, 10, 11
- Improve with time
- Malefics do well here

### <span className="hindi">मारक</span> (Maraka) - Death-inflicting Houses
Houses: 2, 7
- Can cause health issues during dashas
