* **4th house** = happiness, property, mother, vehicles, agriculture, comforts.

* Natural lords **Moon and Venus** have **digbala (directional strength)** here → very powerful.

* When the **4th lord is in the 4th house**:

  * Extremely auspicious placement.
  * Very good for **property, happiness, mother, comforts, and stability**.
  * Native may **rise to high position (even ministership)**.
  * Will possess **wealth, skills, virtues, honor, learning, and happiness**.
  * Strongly disposed toward spouse because:

    * In Kal Purusha chart, Venus is 7th lord and karaka for wife.
    * So 4L in 4H often shows **happiness through wife/partner**.

* **Professional/career impact:**

  * 4H directly aspects the **10th house of karma**.
  * When 4L is in 4H (especially in own sign), native finds **happiness in work**.
  * Doing work with happiness brings **great skill and success**.

* **Agriculture/property:**

  * Since 4H rules agriculture, the person may succeed in **farming or land-related work**.

* **Contentment:**

  * Even if not extremely wealthy, people with this placement remain **calm, composed, and content**.
  * Having house, some land, a car, and family comforts keeps them happy.
  * If exalted, they need very little else in life and are always satisfied.

---

✅ **In short:**

* **4th lord in 4th house** is one of the **best placements**.
* Brings strong happiness from mother, spouse, property, vehicles, comforts, and career.
* Native is naturally calm, content, skillful, and prosperous.
