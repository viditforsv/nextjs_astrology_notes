# Welcome to Vedic Astrology Notes

Welcome to your comprehensive guide to Vedic Astrology (Jyotish Shastra). This documentation covers the fundamental principles, concepts, and techniques of this ancient Indian system of astrology.

## What is Vedic Astrology?

Vedic Astrology, also known as Jyotish (the science of light), is an ancient system of astrology that originated in India thousands of years ago. It is based on the Vedas, the oldest sacred texts of Hinduism.

## Key Features

- **Sidereal Zodiac**: Uses the actual positions of constellations
- **Nakshatras**: 27 lunar mansions that provide deeper insights
- **Dashas**: Planetary periods that predict timing of events
- **Divisional Charts**: Multiple chart divisions for detailed analysis
- **Remedial Measures**: Practical solutions through mantras, gemstones, and rituals
