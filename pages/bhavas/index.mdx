# Bhavas (Houses)

The birth chart is divided into twelve houses (Bhavas), each representing different areas of life.

## Classification of Houses

### <span className="hindi">केंद्र स्थान</span> (Kendra Sthan) - Angular Houses

**Houses: 1, 4, 7, 10**

- <span className="hindi">विष्णु स्थान</span> (Vishnu Sthans) - Important bhavas
- These are the most important houses in the chart
- **1st House (Lagna)**: Is both <span className="hindi">केंद्र</span> (Kendra) and <span className="hindi">त्रिकोण</span> (Trikona)

### <span className="hindi">त्रिकोण स्थान</span> (Trikona Sthana) - Trinal Houses

**Houses: 1, 5, 9**

- <span className="hindi">लक्ष्मी स्थान</span> (Laxmi Sthan) - Auspicious houses
- <span className="hindi">शुभ स्थान</span> (Shubh Sthans) - They are auspicious houses
- **Purification Power**: The lords of 1, 5, 9 can purify any planet they associate with, even if that lord is the lord of 6, 8, or 12
- **1, 5, 10**: Are houses of <span className="hindi">कृपा</span> of <span className="hindi">राजा</span> (Kripa of Raja) - Grace of the King

### <span className="hindi">त्रिषडाय</span> (Trisadaya) - Malefic Houses

**Houses: 6, 8, 12**

- These houses have negative energy
- They can pollute other planets that come in contact with them
- Planets placed here or their lords can bring challenges and obstacles
