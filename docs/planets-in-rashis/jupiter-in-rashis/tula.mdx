## Key Factors

### Friend Relationship
- <span className="hindi">गुरु</span> and <span className="hindi">शुक्र</span> are **friends**
- Jupiter (wisdom, dharma) + Venus (balance, relationships) = diplomatic wisdom
- Creates:
  - Balanced spirituality
  - Diplomatic teaching
  - Harmonious dharma
  - Fair and just guru
  - Relationship-oriented wisdom
  - Social grace with ethics
- Wisdom with harmony

### Movable Sign Nature
- <span className="hindi">तुला</span> is **Chara (Movable) Rashi**
- Movable air = constantly seeking balance, social movement
- Jupiter's expansion through relationships
- Creates: Diplomatic expansion, social dharma, relationship-focused growth

### Lordship Impact (3rd and 6th Lord in 1st)

**3rd Lord in 1st House:**
- Self-made person through own efforts
- Communication and courage define personality
- Courageous and initiative-taking nature
- Skills and hobbies central to identity
- Good relationship with younger siblings
- Short travels frequently
- Hands-on approach to life
- Media, writing, teaching career
- Brave in expressing dharma

**6th Lord in 1st House:**
- Service-oriented personality
- Competitive and hardworking nature
- Health-conscious
- Overcomes enemies through self
- Daily work defines identity
- May face health obstacles
- Strong work ethic
- Problem-solver mentality
- Good for service professions
- Dharma through service

**Combined Effect (3L + 6L in 1H):**
- Courage in service
- Self-made through hard work (3H effort + 6H service)
- Communication about service and health
- Overcomes obstacles through own courage
- Skills used in service professions
- Siblings may be in service sector
- **Excellent for service-oriented teaching, counseling**
