1. **General Nature**

   * 8th house = death, longevity, secrets, in-laws, past-life karmas.
   * Mars is significator of death (*maran karaka*) → very intense placement.

2. **Effect on Spouse**

   * Very bad for spouse’s health.
   * Spouse may suffer from chronic illness; if Mars afflicted with malefics, can cause spouse’s early death.

3. **Secretive Nature**

   * Person becomes very secretive, keeps things hidden.
   * Mars here strengthens ability to conceal, execute silently.
   * With Venus → strong tendency for secret or extra-marital affairs, hidden but long-lasting.

4. **Property & Wealth**

   * Can give ancestral property if Mars is exalted/own/friendly sign.
   * If debilitated, person may reject parental property.
   * Well-placed → gives immense, even unprecedented property and wealth.

5. **Longevity & Death**

   * 8th house shows type of death.
   * Mars here often indicates accidental, violent, or sudden death (road accident, surgery, blood loss, or even murder if with Rahu).
   * Element decides form:

     * Water sign → death by drowning/liquid-related.
     * Air/char sign → accident-related.
   * Operation table death also possible.

6. **In-Laws**

   * Creates problems with in-laws.
   * If afflicted → violence from in-laws.
   * If neutral → indifference, no close relationship.

7. **Spiritual/Karmic Dimension**

   * Any planet in 8th is tied to past-life karmas (*nij dosha*).
   * Mars here = misuse of power in past life → in this birth, struggle for power, denial of authority.
   * For Sagittarius ascendant (5th lord Mars in 8th) → interest in mantra-sadhana and occult practices.
