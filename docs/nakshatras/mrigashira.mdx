## Characteristics

Mrigashira represents the constant search for knowledge and new experiences. Like a deer, natives are gentle, curious, and always alert.

### Positive Traits
- Curious and inquisitive mind
- Gentle and soft-spoken
- Artistic and creative
- Good communication skills
- Adaptable and flexible
- Intelligent and perceptive
- Love for travel and exploration
- Charming personality

### Negative Traits
- Restless and fickle-minded
- Suspicious and doubtful
- Can be timid or fearful
- Difficulty making decisions
- May lack consistency
- Overly sensitive

## Special Notes

Mrigashira spans both Taurus and Gemini, giving it a unique blend of earthy stability and airy communication. It's considered good for:
- Starting education
- Creative projects
- Making jewelry
- Planting gardens
- Beginning searches or investigations

The deer symbolism makes this nakshatra particularly restless but also graceful and beautiful. Lord Chandra (Moon) was born in this nakshatra.
