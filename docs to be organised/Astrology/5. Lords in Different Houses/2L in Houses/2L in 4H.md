### 4th House
- Wealth is generated via properties etc. of 4H things
- Buy things of 4H
- They know how to use money. 
- Happiness from food, from family money etc. 
- Person can get inheritence 
- Benefic
	- Truthful, speaking from heart
	- Kind person
- Malefic if not in swarashi or uccha
	- Ruthless, unkind
- Earn through public
- Drishti on 10H, 
	- Work into property dept of bank
- If in conjunction with jupiter or exalted, native will be equal to a king. 
	- High profile, related to king. 
- Jupiter is responsible for Raj kripa
- Ve and Ju energy in 4th house. 
- Ve and Mo get Digbal in 4H

1. **Basic Meaning**

   * 2nd = wealth.
   * 4th = property, mother, agriculture, conveyances, land, education, vehicles, happiness.
   * So, 2nd lord in 4th → wealth connected to property, vehicles, land, agriculture, and general happiness.

2. **Use of Money**

   * In 1st house → focus is on saving money.
   * In 4th house → knowing how to *enjoy* money.
   * Person uses wealth to buy property, cars, food, travel, comfort, relationships.
   * Real use of money: exchanging it for happiness.

3. **Family/Inheritance**

   * 2nd house = family. Lord in 4th → wealth and property through family, inheritance, ancestral property.

4. **Speech & Heart Connection**

   * 2nd = speech, 4th = heart/mind.
   * If benefic: speech comes from the heart → truthful, kind, generous.
   * If malefic (not exalted or in own sign): person may be ruthless, unkind in financial matters.

5. **Public Connection**

   * 4th = house of public.
   * 2nd lord in 4th → person can earn through public-related work (doctor, astrologer, actor, self-employed, etc.).
   * Not everyone earns directly through public (some do B2B), but here focus is on public-facing earnings.

6. **Aspect on 10th House**

   * If 2nd lord is strong in 4th and aspects 10th → person may work in property departments, banks (financial instruments + property), car division, etc.
   * Links wealth, property, and profession.

7. **Classical Reference**

   * Parashara: 2nd lord in 4th = wonderful placement.
   * Person endowed with all kinds of wealth.
   * If conjunct Jupiter (dhana karaka, rajya-kripa): equal to a king, can reach high posts, have political/high-profile connections.
   * Jupiter, Venus, Moon in 4th = strong dhana yoga (since Venus & Moon also get digbala in 4th).

8. **Exaltation/Debility**

   * Exalted 2nd lord in 4th → person lives like a king.
   * Debilitated in 4th → still a good placement, gives happiness, though spending pattern depends on 12th lord.

9. **Conclusion**

   * Overall: 2nd lord in 4th is a **very good placement**.
   * Grants wealth, happiness, property, comforts, family prosperity, and use of money for joy.
