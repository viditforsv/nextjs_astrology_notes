## Jupiter's Aspects

Regardless of rashi placement, <span className="hindi">गुरु</span> always aspects:
- **5th House** from itself (Intelligence, Children, Creativity)
- **7th House** from itself (Partnerships, Marriage, Public)
- **9th House** from itself (Fortune, Dharma, Father)

## Understanding the Table

**State:**
- ⭐ **Exaltation (Uchcha)**: Maximum power (Cancer only)
- ⭐ **Own Sign (Swakshetra)**: Pure expression (Sagittarius, Pisces)
- ⚠️ **Debilitation (Neecha)**: Weakest power (Capricorn only)
- **Friend/Enemy/Neutral**: Relationship with rashi lord

**Lordship (when Jupiter is in 1st House):**
- Shows which houses Jupiter rules for that lagna
- Example: For Aries lagna, Jupiter rules 9th (Sagittarius) and 12th (Pisces) houses

**Special Yogas:**
- **Raja Yoga**: 10th lord combinations (career success, authority)
- **Dhana Yoga**: 2nd, 5th, 11th lord combinations (wealth)
- **Bhagya Yoga**: 9th lord in 1st (fortune)
