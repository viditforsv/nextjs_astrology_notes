- 1. **General Nature**

   * Known as *Shatru Hanta* (destroyer of enemies).
   * Mars here makes the native unbeatable against opponents.
   * Excellent placement for sports, police, army, defense, and even medical profession (where struggle and fight are required).

2. **Aspects & Influence**

   * Mars aspects Lagna → gives power, valor, strength.
   * Mars aspects 12th house → creates victory even with expenses or struggles.

3. **Power & Authority**

   * Strong Mars in 6th = power to rule, dominate, and win.
   * Illustrates that true rulership requires strength and force, not love alone.
   * Only those with strong Mars can truly command authority.

4. **If Debilitated or Afflicted**

   * Debilitated Mars in 6th (Cancer sign) can misuse power.
   * Creates constant conflicts, both external (with others) and internal (ego, anger).
   * Karma becomes tied to quarrels and disputes.

5. **Inner Enemies (Shadripu)**

   * 6th house represents six internal weaknesses (lust, anger, greed, attachment, pride, jealousy).
   * Mars here amplifies ego and anger.
   * If exalted, person has immense confidence, sometimes leading to arrogance.

6. **Overall Outcome**

   * Generally favorable placement for Mars since it strengthens courage and destroys enemies.
   * But if ill-placed, can make the person quarrelsome, domineering, and egoistic.
   * Success comes, but with stress and conflicts.
