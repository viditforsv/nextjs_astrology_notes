# Quick Reference Notes

Concise notes and reminders for various astrological concepts.

## Dasha Lord Influence Areas

1. **Lordship** - Which houses it rules
2. **Position** - Which house it sits in
3. **<span className="hindi">दृष्टि</span> (Drishti)** - Which houses it aspects
4. **Aspected by Planets** - Which planets aspect it
5. **Nakshatra Lord** - Whose nakshatra + its lordship (important in Gochar)
6. **Conjunction** - Planets conjunct with it
7. **6-8-12 from Dasha Lord** - Negative results from these houses
8. **2H & 7H** - Maarak (death-giving) results
9. **Kendra-Trikona from Position** - Positive results

## Key Principles

### Placement vs. Drishti
**ग्रह जहाँ बैठा है वो long lasting होती है, जहाँ दृष्टि होती है वो आता जाता रहता है**
- Where planet sits = Long-lasting results
- Where planet aspects = Temporary, comes and goes

### Nakshatra in Dasha
**Very important:** Planet sitting in which nakshatra + nakshatra lord position + lordship

### 6-8-12 from Dasha Lord Position
**दशा lord जिस भी स्थान से 6, 8, 12 बैठा है, उसके negative results आएंगे**
- Example: <span className="hindi">राहु</span> in 5H → Negative results of 6H, 10H, 12H
- But: 5H (trikona) quality also gives some positive results

### Kendra-Trikona from Dasha Lord
**दशा lord की placement से kendra-trikona के positive results आएंगे**
- Example: <span className="hindi">राहु</span> in 5H → Good results from 9H, 1H, 8H, 11H, 2H

### 6-8-12 Lord Impact
**Lords sitting 6-8-12 from dasha lord will not allow it to work**
- They obstruct the dasha lord's functioning

### 2H & 7H Maarak
- **2H & 7H से मारक के result देगा**
- Critical for longevity analysis

## Maarak (Death-Giving) Positions

### 10H Planet Dasha
**Mother-Father death** - Maarak to both parents

### 8H Planet Dasha
**Spouse death** - 8H is 2nd from 7H (maarak)

### Spouse Death Planets
- **2H planet** - 8th from 7H
- **1H planet** - 7th from 7H
- **8H planet** - 2nd from 7H

**पाप ग्रह बैठ जाएगा तो मृत्यु होगी**
- If malefic sits, death occurs
- If benefic sits as maarak, person goes peacefully

### 7H Note
**7H is both Kendra and Maarak**

## Mahadasha-Antardasha-Pratyantara Relationship

### Hierarchy
**MD → AD → PD**
- Mahadasha lord dependent on Antardasha lord
- Antardasha lord dependent on Pratyantara lord
- This hierarchy must be followed

### MD & AD Relationship
- **Friends + good placement** = Good results
- **6-8 or bad orientation** = Results suffer

### Bridge Concept
**If MD and PD are good, but AD is bad:**
- Person will still face MD issues
- **AD की permission के बिना काम नहीं होगा** - Work won't happen without AD's permission
- AD is the bridge

## Remedies

### Two Types
1. **Chart-based** - Based on birth chart
2. **Dasha-based** - Based on current dasha period

### 3 Ms for Remedies
1. **Meditation**
2. **Medication**
3. **Mantra**

### Health & Death-Related
**Maha Mrityunjaya Mantra**
- 100% cure not guaranteed, but helps

### Exchange (Parivartana)
**पाप ग्रह exchange में छोड़ देते हैं**
- Don't engage in malefic exchange activities
- More you engage, more suffering (<span className="hindi">कष्ट</span>) comes
- If you pick, take their positives only
