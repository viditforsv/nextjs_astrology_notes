### Interesting Facts
- Extremely thin exosphere ‚Äì practically no atmosphere
- Experiences moonquakes caused by tidal stresses from Earth
- Albedo (Reflectivity): 0.12 (reflects only 12% of sunlight)
- No light of its own - reflects sunlight
- Formation: Giant Impact Hypothesis (debris from collision with Theia)
- Causes ocean tides on Earth due to gravitational pull
- The Moon is slowly moving away from Earth: ~3.8 cm/year
- You always see the same face of the Moon from Earth
- No magnetic field
- Fifth-largest moon in the Solar System

### Exploration
- First human landing: Apollo 11 (Neil Armstrong, 1969)
- Moon rocks brought to Earth: ~382 kg by NASA missions
