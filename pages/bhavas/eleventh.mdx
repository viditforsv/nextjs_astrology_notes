# 11th Bhava - <span className="hindi">लाभ भाव</span> (Labh Bhava)

## Basic Information

- **Sanskrit Name**: <span className="hindi">लाभ भाव</span> (Labh Bhava - House of Income, Desires)
- **Significator**: <span className="hindi">शनि</span> (Saturn), <span className="hindi">राहु</span> (Rahu)
- **Classification**: <span className="hindi">उपचय</span> (Upachaya/Growth House)
- **Essence**: House of "Gains & Desire Fulfillment"
- **Planet**: Ruled by **<span className="hindi">शनि</span> (Saturn)** (Aquarius) + co-lord **<span className="hindi">राहु</span> (Rahu)**
- **Note**: Income ≠ Wealth. Wealth = Income – Expenses (→ 2nd House). Free will here — your effort decides income
