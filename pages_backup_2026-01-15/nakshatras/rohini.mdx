## Characteristics

Rohini is considered the most beautiful and fertile of all nakshatras. It represents growth, fertility, and material abundance.

### Positive Traits
- Extremely attractive and charming
- Artistic and creative
- Nurturing and caring
- Materially prosperous
- Stable and grounded
- Good taste in aesthetics
- Strong emotional connections
- Excellent in business

### Negative Traits
- Can be overly materialistic
- Possessive and jealous
- Stubborn and inflexible
- May be self-indulgent
- Critical of others
- Can be manipulative for gain

## Special Notes

Rohini is Lord Krishna's birth nakshatra, making it highly auspicious. It's excellent for:
- Starting new businesses
- Marriage ceremonies
- Purchasing property or vehicles
- Creative projects
- Fertility treatments

The Moon is said to be most attached to Rohini among all 27 wives (nakshatras), making it especially powerful for lunar activities and emotional fulfillment.
