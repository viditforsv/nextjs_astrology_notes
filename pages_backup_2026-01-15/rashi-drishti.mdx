# Rashi Drishti (Sign Aspects)

Rashi Drishti refers to the aspect relationship between signs, where entire signs aspect each other regardless of planetary positions.

---

## Overview

Unlike planetary aspects (graha drishti), rashi drishti is based on the relationship between signs themselves.

## Key Principles

- Signs have natural aspect relationships
- All planets in an aspecting sign influence the aspected sign
- Works in conjunction with planetary aspects
- Important for house significations

## Sign Aspect Relationships

Signs aspect each other based on their natural relationships in the zodiac:

- **7th Aspect**: Opposite signs (full aspect)
- **3rd Aspect**: Signs 3 positions away
- **9th Aspect**: Signs 9 positions away
- **10th Aspect**: Signs 10 positions away

## Application

- Used in chart analysis alongside planetary aspects
- Affects house significations
- Important in timing predictions
- Influences planetary combinations
