* **4th house** = happiness, mind, property, comforts.
* **5th house** = children, intellect, mantra, creativity.
* **Relation**: 5th is the **2nd (sustenance) from 4th** → it sustains happiness.

### Key Results:

* **General effect**:

  * Very **wonderful combination** (kendra + trikona, Vishnu + Lakshmi).
  * Happiness is **sustained** and long-lasting.
  * Native becomes **happy and fortunate**.

* **When 4th lord goes to 5th house**:

  * Happiness flows into **intellect, mantra, devotion, creativity, children**.
  * Mind (4H) finds stability through mantra (5H).
  * Person can become a **devotee**, inclined towards **bhakti** (especially if Moon is involved).
  * Happiness maintained either through **wisdom/intellect** or through **mantra and devotion**.

* **If Moon as 4L in 5H**:

  * Strong focus on children.
  * Brings devotion, bhakti, healing qualities.
  * Makes person more emotional and nurturing.

* **If Venus as 4L in 5H**:

  * Enhances creativity, attractiveness, and artistic expression.
  * Happiness found in love, children, and creativity.

* **If Mars is the 4L in 5H**:

  * Placement is good, but Mars in 5H can bring **anger issues**.

---

✅ **In short:**

* **4th lord in 5th house** sustains happiness through **children, intellect, mantra, devotion, and creativity**.
* Brings **long-lasting joy, fortune, and devotion**.
* If benefic → very auspicious (devotional, creative, attractive).
* If malefic like Mars → may bring anger but still strong energy in creativity/children matters.
