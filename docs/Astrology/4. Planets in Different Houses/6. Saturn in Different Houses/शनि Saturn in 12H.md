**Saturn in the 12th House**

* **House Significations:**

  * 12th house = expenditure, loss, isolation, foreign lands, ashrams, moksha, imprisonment.
  * Saturn is the natural significator (karaka) of the 12th house.

* **General effects:**

  * Being karaka for the 12th, Saturn here can push the native toward wrongful acts if afflicted.
  * Often creates physical problems; 7th aspect falls on the 6th house (disease, service, disputes).

* **If benefic influences present:**

  * Can keep the person content and maintain eyesight.
  * With Jupiter or Mercury → tendency toward spiritual merit, possibility of “going to heaven” (positive karmic end).
  * With Rahu → negative outcomes, even “naraka” (spiritual downfall).

* **Expenditure patterns:**

  * If charity/donation is avoided, expenses may occur through illness.
  * 12th Saturn is generally not considered auspicious; also said to reduce intellect.

* **Aspects & consequences:**

  * 7th aspect on 6th house influences health and service matters.
  * Aspect on 2nd house (if 12th lord Saturn) can cause government or tax authorities to take money.

* **Foreign & spiritual connections:**

  * Strong potential for foreign travel or settlement, especially if supported by other combinations.
  * Well-placed (exalted or friendly sign) Saturn can lead to living in foreign lands or in ashrams.
  * Good Jupiter with 12th Saturn → likely ashram life or spiritual retreat.

* **Afflicted combinations:**

  * With Rahu or Mars → criminal tendencies, imprisonment, court cases (jail yoga).
