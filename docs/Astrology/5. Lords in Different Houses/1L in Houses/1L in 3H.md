### 3rd House
[[3H - Parakram Bhava]] - Buddha ka house in Kaal Purush Chart
- Brave like lion, fighter
- All kinds of wealth because of valour
- Intelligent, happy, many wives
- Lagnesh is always into effort
- If ill placed - kaam chor
- Benefic
	- Mathematician, 
	- Arts and creative fields
- eg: Mercury, Moon in 3H, drishti of malefic, native will be a coward avoid fight, Mercury will want to fight with pen. 
- eg: Jupiter - 3H is maran karaka sthan for Ju, will make the person spiritual because of Drishti on 9H
- eg: Sa - 3H, person will not be spiritual but brave. 
	- Saturn will make the person unlucky
- eg: Me - Pen in hand
- eg: Ma - Sword of gun in hand
