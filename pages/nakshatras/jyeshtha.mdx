## Characteristics

Jyeshtha represents seniority, authority, and protective power. It's the "eldest" nakshatra, associated with responsibility and the burden of leadership.

### Positive Traits
- Natural leadership abilities
- Protective and responsible
- Courageous and bold
- Charitable and generous
- Self-reliant and independent
- Strong willpower
- Intellectual and analytical
- Excellent at managing crises

### Negative Traits
- Can be arrogant and domineering
- May have superiority complex
- Prone to anger and irritability
- Can be hypocritical
- Difficulty accepting authority
- May be suspicious and secretive
- Tendency toward self-destruction

## Special Notes

Jyeshtha is a complex and powerful nakshatra. The earring or circular amulet represents protection and authority. The umbrella symbolizes protection offered to others.

This nakshatra is challenging because it brings:
- Great power with great responsibility
- Leadership that can become burdensome
- Tendency to bear others' problems
- Need to balance pride with humility

Jyeshtha is NOT generally considered auspicious for:
- Starting new ventures
- Marriages
- Beginning education
- Peaceful activities

However, it IS good for:
- Protective rituals
- Dealing with enemies
- Military operations
- Taking charge in emergencies

People born under Jyeshtha often experience a "fall before rise" pattern - they may face significant challenges early in life but eventually rise to positions of authority and respect. The key lesson is to use power wisely and without arrogance.
