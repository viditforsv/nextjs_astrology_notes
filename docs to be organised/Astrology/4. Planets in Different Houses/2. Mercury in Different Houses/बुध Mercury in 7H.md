**Summary – Mercury in the 7th House (Transcript Only)**

* **House significance**:

  * 7th house = marriage, partnerships (including business), spouse, physical relationships.
  * Mercury is a generally benefic planet, but its results depend heavily on association (*sangat*).

* **Marriage & relationships**:

  * Placement usually brings **problems in marriage**.
  * Being a **neutral/napunsak** planet in Venus’s house (7th), it can **reduce potency** and affect physical relationships.
  * Mercury’s **dual nature** can create instability in marriage and possibility of **two marriages** if afflicted.
  * Badly placed Mercury can cause **major marital problems**, **divorce**, or **multiple marriages**.
  * In a woman’s chart, Mercury in the 7th can indicate that the **husband/spouse may lack potency**.

* **Nature of spouse**:

  * Generally, the wife (or spouse) is intelligent and beautiful.

* **Business & career effects**:

  * Mercury in 7th aspects the *lagna* (1st house) → increases intelligence and ability to implement ideas.
  * Strong inclination towards **business**, using mental abilities and education for commercial gains.
  * Good placement for **business partnerships**.

* **Physical appearance**:

  * Natives often **look younger** than their actual age.

* **Overall judgment**:

  * **Good for**: business partnerships, intelligence, youthful looks.
  * **Bad for**: marriage stability, physical relationship harmony (especially if afflicted).
