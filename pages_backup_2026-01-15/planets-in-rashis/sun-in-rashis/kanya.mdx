- Detachment from worldly attachments
- Transformative, ascetic tendencies

- Isolation tendency

## Remedies & Integration

### Strengthen Weakened Sun

1. **Shaucha (Purity)** - Maintain extreme cleanliness, fix agni
2. **Tapas (Discipline)** - Service as spiritual practice
3. **Ishvara Pranidhana (Surrender)** - Surrender perfectionism to divine
4. **Santosha (Contentment)** - Accept imperfection
5. **Brahmacharya (Moderation)** - Balance criticism

### Practical Remedies
- **Surya Namaskar** to strengthen Sun
- **Gayatri Mantra** at sunrise
- Offer water to Sun
- Ruby gemstone (strengthen weak Sun)
- Fast on Sundays
- **Digestive fire remedies:**
  - **अग्नि दीपक** - Digestive enzymes
  - Ginger, black pepper, cumin
  - Regular meal times
  - Avoid cold foods
- **Migraine management:**
  - Stress reduction
  - Regular sleep
  - Hydration
  - Head massage
- **Balance criticism** with gratitude
- Serve others without ego
- Respect father (always supportive)
- **Children's health priority** - regular check-ups
